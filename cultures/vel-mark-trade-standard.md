# VEL Mark Trade Standard

## Overview

VEL Mark Trade Standard is the dominant currency convention used for most day-to-day and contract-level trade across F432.

## Beliefs and Values

Inhabitants across Nauw, Wabet, and Sorel treat VEL Marks as the most practical shared medium for pricing, wages, and routine market settlement. The standard is valued less as ideology and more as a stability tool: one reference unit that reduces friction between regions with different local customs.

## Practices and Traditions

Many settlements and guild circles still maintain local tender forms, vouchers, or exchange customs for neighborhood-scale trade. In practice, however, larger basket pricing, inter-regional cargo accounting, and most civic fee schedules are commonly quoted in VEL.

Common speech shortens the denomination language to "vel" or "vel marks" in market conversation.

## Lore

VEL Marks are not the only currency structure in Dias. Beyond F432, some frequencies settle value through non-note systems, including resource-weighted exchange anchored to critical minerals, rare materials, or other strategic goods. Some cross-frequency flows also rely on non-physical settlement channels instead of paper notes.

Within F432, VEL remains the dominant trusted physical trade standard used by most inhabitants.

## Notes

This entry defines broad usage behavior, not a monopoly claim. Local and cross-frequency variation remains canonical.
