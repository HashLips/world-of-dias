# Sorel Exile Compact

## Overview

Sorel Exile Compact is a shared frontier culture in which belonging is earned through endurance, contribution, and pact-keeping rather than origin status.

## Beliefs and Values

The compact rejects imposed hierarchy from outside Sorel while still enforcing internal expectations around loyalty, debt memory, and survival reciprocity.

People are judged by what they carry, keep, and return to the collective under stress.

## Practices and Traditions

Pact language, masked identity signaling, camp-to-camp oath carrying, and negotiated protection lines are common social practices across both Hearthvale and Driftfall variants.

## Lore

Although locally diverse, the compact is widely associated with Carrow Vale's rise and with the transformation of exile identity into shared political structure.

## Notes

Hearthvale versions trend restorative; Driftfall versions trend volatile and opportunistic.
