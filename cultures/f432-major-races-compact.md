# F432 Major Races Compact

## Overview

The **F432 Major Races Compact** is a civic framing term used by scholars, census houses, and story archivists for the seven most commonly encountered races across the F432 realm: **Humans**, **Axiomorphs**, **Veyrin**, **Lineborn**, **Kharad**, **Choirglass**, and **Palewind Swarms**.

## Beliefs and Values

The compact is descriptive, not hierarchical. It does not claim superiority of any race; it records which groups are most visible in trade routes, governance documents, labor systems, and narrative history in Nauw, Wabet, and Sorel.

The framework also assumes that F432 is a **high-contact realm**. Many additional races pass through, settle in minority pockets, or appear in rotating enclaves linked to frequency drift and long-distance migration.

## Practices and Traditions

In civic use, compact terminology supports translation standards, census tags, route safety notes, and inter-house mediation protocols. In story use, it helps writers establish baseline social composition without implying a closed species list for Dias.

## Lore

Other frequencies host races not yet fully documented in this repository. F432 institutions therefore treat any seven-race list as a **current major roster**, not a final cosmic inventory.
