# Veloria Street Flower Pot

## Overview

A common floral display vessel used across Veloria City, usually holding vibrant yellow blossoms seen from the outer ring to the inner districts.

## Description

These pots appear along streets, market lanes, storefronts, and ring-side walkways throughout Veloria. Though individually simple, they function as a repeated visual marker of care and color in daily city life.

The most recognized arrangement features bright yellow flowers in dense clusters, widely associated with public warmth and neighborhood pride.

## Cultural Meaning

Residents often read the ringwide presence of these flower pots as a subtle symbol that, despite technological differences between city bands, shared civic identity still exists across Veloria.

## Usage

Sold and maintained through street vendors and flower shops distributed across the city's rings; used for storefront decoration, household entrances, and public-facing walkways.

## Notes

Forms and materials vary by district, but the yellow bloom arrangement is treated as the classic Veloria street style.
