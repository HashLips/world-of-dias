# Golden Tear Vials

## Overview

Golden Tear Vials are sealed vessels said to hold preserved droplets linked to Sael Corvin's golden-tear events.

## Description

Each vial is small, tightly sealed, and handled under cloister protocol. Public access is highly restricted, and provenance claims are often indirect.

The objects are cataloged as warning-class relics in Averra lore circles, not as ordinary devotional keepsakes.

## Cultural Meaning

Believers treat the vials as custody markers for moments when important truth approached erasure. Skeptics treat them as curated theater reinforcing cloister authority.

## Usage

They are used in closed ritual review contexts and archive-sealing ceremonies associated with memory-risk periods.

## Notes

No public verification confirms composition, age, or continuity of stored material.
