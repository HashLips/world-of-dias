# Prime Relics

## Overview

Prime Relics are objects and remnants attributed to the Prime Age that exhibit properties modern civilizations cannot replicate.

## Description

Prime Relics are often identified by one or more of the following traits:

- functioning across multiple Frequencies
- behaving consistently despite differing local laws of reality
- emitting resonance patterns that do not match known frequency signatures
- containing symbols repeated in ruins found in distant Frequencies

Because of these traits, many scholars believe the Prime Realm possessed knowledge or technologies far beyond what exists in the Present Age.

## Cultural Meaning

Prime Relics are simultaneously scholarly obsession, religious proof, and political leverage. To some, they are evidence that the Prime Realm can be understood—or restored. To others, they are warnings that whatever preceded the Fracture should remain buried.

## Usage

Most Prime Relics cannot be reliably activated or controlled. They are typically studied, contained, traded, or hidden, depending on the culture and the risks involved.

## Notes

“Prime Relic” is a classification applied after the Fracture; provenance is rarely certain.
