# Blue Rose Vow

## Overview

Blue Rose Vow refers to a kept blue rose, natural or crafted, used as a personal commitment object during periods of loss, uncertainty, or exile transition.

## Description

The object is commonly represented as a blue bloom held upright even while stem and petals begin to bend. The visual grammar signals strain without surrender.

## Cultural Meaning

In Sorel-linked interpretation, the Blue Rose Vow marks a promise to continue acting toward a chosen future despite grief or immediate deterioration of conditions.

## Usage

Used in private vows, threshold departures, and return promises. In some circles, the rose is carried until either reunion or deliberate release at a recognized crossing point.

## Notes

The concept is symbolic practice first and material specification second.
