# Exile Mask of Carrow Vale

## Overview

The Exile Mask of Carrow Vale is the face-covering object linked to the First Exile's rise in Sorel.

## Description

The mask is treated less as apparel and more as a permanent political object: the point at which a former identity is closed and a frontier authority begins.

Public depictions preserve silhouette and presence but avoid agreed facial detail, reinforcing the mask's role as active concealment.

## Cultural Meaning

In Sorel speech, the mask signifies irreversibility. To followers it marks survival transformed into command; to detractors it marks fear institutionalized as leadership.

## Usage

The mask is invoked in oath language, faction rhetoric, and legitimacy claims tied to exile lineage.

## Notes

No verified public record describes Carrow Vale's face after exile.
