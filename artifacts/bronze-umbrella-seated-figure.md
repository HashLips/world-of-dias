# Bronze Umbrella Seated Figure

## Overview

A large bronze statue of a seated man holding an umbrella, positioned at the center of The Sketchy Gallery hallway.

## Description

The sculpture stands directly beneath a circular skylight that casts a soft beam across the figure through most of the day. Its placement interrupts the long corridor with a visual anchor and gathering point.

## Cultural Meaning

Visitors commonly pause at the statue before continuing deeper into the gallery, giving it the role of a reflective midpoint between entry and the unknown blue door at the far end.

## Usage

Installed as a central gallery piece and orienting landmark within the moving-panel exhibition corridor.
