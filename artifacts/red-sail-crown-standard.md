# Red Sail Crown-Standard

## Overview

The Red Sail Crown-Standard is the signature regalia associated with Vailor Quentin's authority over F432.

## Description

The object is worn as a head-standard shaped in a sail-like form, rendered in a vivid red profile recognizable across major regions of the realm.

Its symbolic weight exceeds its material description: in public memory, the object stands for command, continuity, and the right to issue realm-scale direction.

## Cultural Meaning

Supporters treat the crown-standard as proof of Vailor's legitimacy and stabilizing role. Critics invoke its disputed origin as evidence that authority in F432 may begin in force before becoming institution.

## Usage

The crown-standard appears in formal addresses, major declarations, and ceremonial representations tied to realm unity.

## Notes

Its historical acquisition remains disputed between gift and seizure traditions.
