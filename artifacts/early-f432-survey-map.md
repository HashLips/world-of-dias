# Early F432 Survey Map

## Overview

An early cartographic depiction of F432 used as a baseline reference for the realm's large regional layout.

## Description

This map captures an earlier surveyed understanding of F432 and is intentionally treated as incomplete and time-bound. It accurately represents broad regional placement used by current canon—Wabet to the west, Nauw to the north-east, and Sorel to the south-east—while leaving room for future expansion and refinement.

Source image: `assets/f432-map.png`

## Cultural Meaning

The map is valued less as a final authority and more as a foundational record: a shared starting point for navigators, historians, and world-builders tracking how knowledge of F432 changes over time.

## Usage

Used as an "early era" geographic reference in lore documentation; later maps may add newly charted subregions, routes, landmarks, and revised borders.

## Notes

Canon framing: this is an early depiction, not the definitive final map of F432.
