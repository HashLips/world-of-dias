# The Whisper Field at Bloomline

## Premise

A mediation team arrives at a holding cove to settle a trade dispute, but the ground itself answers with a field of talking heads before any formal words are spoken.

## Story

By the time the delegates reached the cove, the air was already thick with voices.

At first they thought the sound came from the crowd. Then necks began to rise from the soft ground in pale arcs, each ending in a face mid-sentence. One laughed too loudly. One repeated a rumor no one had said aloud. One leaned over another and whispered a private fear into open space.

The Tideheart lead lifted her palm and asked everyone to stand still.

"Not yet," she said. "The field is speaking faster than we are."

For twelve minutes no contract was touched. Teams listened as the Converse Heads cycled through accusation, apology, mockery, and relief in restless succession. When the voices finally thinned, the delegates sat, rewrote two terms, and removed one penalty clause both sides had pretended to accept.

At sunset the cove quieted. The last neck lowered into the mud as if no one had ever been there.

The agreement held for three seasons.
