# The Gel Helm That Never Cracked

## Summary

A Brindle myth explains Bren Holloway's cube-bound head as a survival seal from flood-era collapse.

## Myth Text

The tale says a bridge tower failed above Bren during flood season and a reactive gel casing formed around his head before impact could kill him.  
When floodwater fell, the casing remained, clear and unbroken, and Bren resumed command the same day.

To march crews, "never cracked" became a phrase for leadership that does not fracture under strain.

## Notes

Versions differ on whether the gel is engineered residue, unknown substance, or omen.
