# The Fracture Was Engineered

## Summary

Some traditions insist the Fracture was the consequence of a forgotten civilization experimenting with reality—testing the Prime Realm until it split.

## Myth Text

They tell of a people who could hear the seams of the world. In hidden halls they built instruments to pluck at existence, to tune gravity, to lean time forward and back. The Prime Bridge, in these stories, was not a stabilizer but a control rod—an attempt to hold the Prime Realm steady while they pushed it past what it was meant to bear. When the final experiment began, the world did not collapse. It differentiated. It became Frequencies, and the engineers were scattered into their own mistake.

## Notes

Often invoked when Prime Relics behave “too intentionally” to be natural.
