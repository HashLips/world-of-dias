# The Ring Blades of the Gate Knight

## Summary

A gate myth says Havren Pike mastered circular blades in Lorien service before taking Veloria's threshold command.

## Myth Text

The tale claims Havren trained where shields failed and only moving rings could break encirclement.  
When transferred to Veloria's gate, he adapted the same forms for crowd control, breach response, and close defense.

In local saying, "the rings are turning" means gate law is active and hesitation is over.

## Notes

Commonly retold by watch rotations before high-volume entry cycles.
