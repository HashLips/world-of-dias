# The Canopy Truce of Seven Paths

## Summary

An Aelwyn myth says Oryn ended a multi-tribe corridor feud by binding seven path oaths in one night.

## Myth Text

The tale claims Oryn walked each disputed route before moonfall, hearing every side without ruling.  
At dawn he marked seven boundaries and made each elder swear protection of all seven, not just their own.

From then on, "seven paths" became shorthand for peace that survives ownership disputes.

## Notes

Commonly retold during boundary review gatherings.
