# The Warmth That Stayed

## Summary

An F840 myth tells of a settlement that lost nearly everything except one ember, which became the first Hearthline station because people chose to guard it together.

## Myth Text

The tale says the ember survived not through miracle fuel, but rotation: every household took watch, every traveler added kindling, and every child learned relay duty before ceremonial language.

## Notes

Frequently cited as a cultural origin myth for Ember Keeper stewardship norms.
