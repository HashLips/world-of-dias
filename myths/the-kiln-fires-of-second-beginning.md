# The Kiln Fires of Second Beginning

## Summary

A Hearthvale myth says Alwen lit the first rebuilding kilns with wood gathered by former enemies.

## Myth Text

The story claims Alwen refused to assign blame and instead required every faction to carry fuel to a common fire.  
From those kilns came the first shared tools and stove-brick lines of stable settlement life.

In Hearthvale speech, "keeping kiln fire" means choosing reconstruction over revenge.

## Notes

Retold during work-season openings and re-entry vows.
