# The Warden Who Closed the Reefs

## Summary

A Calareth myth says Coris Vale shut the island's reef routes in a single decree cycle.

## Myth Text

The tale says smuggler anchors scarred the reef so deeply that Coris declared immediate closure, posting watchers before dawn and seizing boats that ignored warning lines.  
By season's end, fish runs recovered and illegal routes dissolved.

In island speech, "closing reefs" now means enforcing a limit before damage becomes permanent.

## Notes

Used by preservation groups as a foundational story of Coris's authority.
