# The Wall That Chooses Liars

## Summary

A persistent Glassfold rumor says the eye-wall does not follow movement equally; it settles hardest on those speaking false intent.

## Myth Text

In market retellings, two travelers may walk the same line while only one feels the full weight of the stare. The reason, people say, is simple and terrifying: the wall does not count footsteps, it counts honesty. Some elders warn against testing it with games, because "a lie told under many eyes can follow you into the next window."

## Notes

Used as informal behavior control in tense crossings, especially before negotiations where motive and wording diverge.
