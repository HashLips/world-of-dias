# The Still Pool Vigil

## Summary

A Glasswater myth says Leora can detect danger by watching a still pool before anyone else sees the sky change.

## Myth Text

In the tale, Leora sits at dawn water and reads the first fracture in reflection before cloud, wind, or birds reveal it.  
When she stands, watchers move; when she remains seated, fields continue.

Whether symbolic or literal, the "still pool vigil" became a local expression for disciplined calm in high-risk seasons.

## Notes

Most often repeated by night-watch families around southern dam basins.
