# The Artist Returned from Sorel

## Summary

A recurring rumor claims The Artist disappeared into Sorel and later reappeared in Veloria, calm and unchanged, as if he had simply taken a long walk.

## Myth Text

They say he vanished south for weeks, past routes most city dwellers refuse to take, and came back carrying fresh paint under his nails but no story in his mouth. Some swear he painted in places where people lose names and direction. Others say he never left at all, and that the tale was invented to explain why he can sit so still.

Believers point to his silence as proof; skeptics call it theater. Either way, the rumor spread because he returned with the same relaxed rhythm, as if Sorel had not touched him.

## Notes

Unverified. Treated as local lore attached to The Artist's public mystique.
