# Old Masters of Averra Isle

## Summary

Across Nauw, a persistent story holds that most of the magic worked openly in the Glasswater Fields, Brindle March, and Velorian Basin did not arise locally but was first taught by secretive Old Masters living on Averra Isle, who supposedly trained early practitioners before vanishing or sealing the island.

## Myth Text

They say the glass-bright rivers remember enchantments whispered at their sources, that the march's oldest bridges hold sigils the builders half forgot, and that Veloria's first market-mages learned their trade from envoys who rowed back from Averra with ink still wet in their journals—then swore the isle had no harbor and no masters, only mist. Skeptics call it dock gossip; believers say the mainland's power still flows from that hidden shore.

## Notes

Treated as folklore and rumor, not verified history. Compatible with modern ignorance of present-day conditions on Averra Isle.
