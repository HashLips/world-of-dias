# The Fruit Walker at Your Door

## Summary

A widespread F432 tale claims that when the Fruit Walker appears at your home, the visit signals a non-ordinary turning point, and the Lucky Fruit he offers carries meaning meant specifically for that household.

## Myth Text

They say you do not summon him. You only hear him when he is already there: basket straps creaking, fruit settling into itself, an old man waiting without hurry.

Some homes receive him at dusk, others before sunrise, and no calendar predicts the knock. Families who believe the story say his visits happen most in Wabet and Nauw, and only rarely in Sorel.

He does not explain why he chose a door. He simply offers fruit, often the kind people call Lucky, and leaves the meaning behind for the household to discover in its own time.

Skeptics call it market folklore attached to ordinary traders. Believers call it warning, blessing, or both.

## Notes

No consensus verifies whether the Fruit Walker is a single person, many people folded into one story, or a fully legendary figure.
