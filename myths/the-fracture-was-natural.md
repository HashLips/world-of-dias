# The Fracture Was Natural

## Summary

A widely repeated explanation claims the Prime Realm did not break by hand or war, but by nature—reality reaching a limit and shedding itself into Frequencies the way a living thing molts.

## Myth Text

They say the Prime Realm was too perfect to remain singular. Harmony, held too long, becomes tension; balance becomes a taut string. In the last days of the Prime Age, the world began to hum—soft at first, then louder—until the resonance had nowhere left to go. The Fracture was not a wound but a release: reality splitting into new tones so it could continue to sing.

## Notes

Often used to argue against attempts to “restore” the Prime Realm.
