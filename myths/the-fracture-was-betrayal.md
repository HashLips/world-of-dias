# The Fracture Was Betrayal

## Summary

In some Frequency-spanning tales, the Prime Realm was shattered by conflict: a betrayal or war that struck at the foundations of reality itself.

## Myth Text

They say the Prime Realm had guardians, keepers of balance who tended the laws like a garden. And they say there were others who wanted more—more power, more reach, more worlds than one. The war was not fought with spears or fire but with principles: a clause rewritten, a law inverted, an oath snapped in half. The Prime Bridge cracked first, like a bone under strain. Then the Prime Realm split into Frequencies, each holding a piece of the argument that could no longer be resolved in one world.

## Notes

Common in cultures that frame the Fracture as moral history rather than physics.
