# The Bell That Rings Twice Differently

## Summary

A foundational F120 tale says a bell at the center of the Returning Span rings twice each dawn: once to remind people what returns, and once to remind them it never returns unchanged.

## Myth Text

When apprentices ask why logs need difference marks, elders point to the bell myth. The first strike is familiar enough to comfort; the second is altered enough to demand attention. Those who ignore either note walk confidently into avoidable mistakes.

## Notes

Frequently used as a teaching story in route training and memory ethics.
