# The Mask Kept After Exile

## Summary

This Sorel myth says Carrow Vale's true face vanished from memory the day exile became destiny.

## Myth Text

The telling says Carrow walked out of northern judgment, placed a mask over the old name, and never removed it again.  
Those who claim to have seen beneath it never agree on what they saw: a scarred survivor, a stranger, or no face at all.

In Sorel speech, "to keep the mask" now means refusing return to a former life.

## Notes

Repeated by both Hearthvale and Driftfall circles with different moral framing.
