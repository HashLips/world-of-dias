# The Moon-Market Walk

## Summary

A basin myth says Tamsin can calm a market district simply by walking its full length at night.

## Myth Text

The story holds that when tensions rise, Tamsin walks the moonlit lanes without guards, stopping at fires, stalls, and dock corners until voices lower on their own.  
By sunrise, fights are deferred, bargains resume, and no one can point to a single order she gave.

Whether literal or idealized, the "moon-market walk" became shorthand for soft authority that still produces hard results.

## Notes

Retold most often in districts that favor communal over formal conflict resolution.
