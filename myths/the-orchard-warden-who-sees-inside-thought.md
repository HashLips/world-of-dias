# The Orchard Warden Who Sees Inside Thought

## Summary

A Verdant myth claims Maes Thorn can read intention before speech is formed.

## Myth Text

The story says disputants arrive prepared to deceive, but abandon their lies after meeting Maes's fixed stare.  
Some tellings insist she answers unspoken objections before anyone voices them.

Whether ability or intimidation, the myth sustains her reputation as a leader difficult to outmaneuver.

## Notes

Repeated most often by traders who failed to secure exceptions from orchard boundary law.
