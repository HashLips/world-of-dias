# The Drifting Face of the Dunes

## Summary

A Lumira myth says Iskar's shifting features reflect the desert's refusal to be fully known.

## Myth Text

In one telling, Iskar's face never settles because each hidden water line leaves a different mark on him.  
In another, the dunes themselves rearrange his form to keep outsiders from reading the true map.

Either way, locals repeat that to follow Iskar safely, one must listen to law before looking for shape.

## Notes

Frequently used to justify oasis secrecy and restricted route sharing.
