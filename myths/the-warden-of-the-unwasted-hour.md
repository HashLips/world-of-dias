# The Warden of the Unwasted Hour

## Summary

A border-lane myth says Neris Hale can feel a wasted minute as if it were physical harm.

## Myth Text

In the story, every delay sends a visible tremor through the rows of potted blooms, and Neris resets the lanes before disorder spreads.  
Travelers who arrive late are not expelled, but placed into corrective pacing rounds until rhythm is restored.

From this tale came the saying: "Do not waste the warden's hour."

## Notes

Retold most often by families who make annual ritual stops at the lanes.
