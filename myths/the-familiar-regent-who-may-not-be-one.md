# The Familiar Regent Who May Not Be One

## Summary

This Veloria myth claims that "Oriven Tal" may be more role than person.

## Myth Text

The tale says no one sees Oriven often enough to prove continuity. Each rare appearance is close enough to be accepted, yet distant enough to preserve doubt.  
In some versions, Oriven is a single hidden strategist. In others, the name is a mantle carried by multiple operators to preserve seamless governance and prevent panic.

The myth endures because the city keeps running, whether or not anyone can point to one permanent face behind the title.

## Notes

The story is often invoked when discussing why Vailor Quentin is said to treat Oriven with unusual caution.
