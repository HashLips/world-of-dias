# The Carrying Wings of Nauw

## Name

The Carrying Wings of Nauw

## Summary

A widely repeated rumor claims that during Mega Butterfly migration, certain inhabitants are seized, lifted into the sky, and carried across the region before being dropped far from home.

## Myth Text

People say the largest Mega Butterflies do not choose targets at random. According to the rumor, those taken are usually travelers standing alone on roads between the western coast and the eastern fields when the migration passes overhead.

In one version, a person lifted near Averra Isle may be found days later in Glasswater Fields, alive but disoriented, unable to explain the journey except for wind, wing-shadow, and long silence above the land.

Some storytellers frame the carrying as punishment for disrespecting migration season. Others insist it is neither cruel nor kind, only instinct: the butterfly treating a human as stray weight caught in its route.

No complete record verifies these stories, but the tale persists strongly enough that many households issue migration-season warnings to avoid open roads when giant wings are sighted.

## Notes

The rumor is often told alongside depictions of Mega Butterfly migration, including *Between Two Places*.
