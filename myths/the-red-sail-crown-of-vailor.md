# The Red Sail Crown of Vailor

## Summary

A widespread F432 myth explains how Vailor Quentin acquired the red-sail crown-standard that defines his rule.

## Myth Text

One telling says Vailor took the sail by overpowering a Singing Fisherman in the early years of his ascent, making dominance itself the first law of his authority.  
Another telling says the same sail was freely gifted to him by maritime elders who believed he alone could hold F432 together.

Because both versions persist with equal conviction, the emblem remains powerful precisely because its origin cannot be settled.

## Notes

This myth is used by both supporters and critics to justify opposite readings of Vailor's legitimacy.
