# The Steward at the Crossing Tables

## Summary

This Nauw myth says Serik Dovant ended a major corridor conflict through one night of forced negotiation.

## Myth Text

The tale claims Serik locked rival road houses, caravan captains, and basin factors at a set of crossing tables until every line of a route-peace charter was signed.  
By dawn, wagons moved again, toll fires were lowered, and food convoys resumed.

Whether literal or not, the phrase "crossing tables" now means a hard compromise no side fully likes but all sides must keep.

## Notes

Frequently cited as the origin story of Serik's authority style.
