# The Fracture Was Over-Resonance

## Summary

This explanation claims the Prime Realm did not break from outside pressure but from within—its resonance intensifying beyond what a single reality could hold.

## Myth Text

They say the Prime Realm learned itself too well. Every thought, every footstep, every birth and death added to the hum until the world’s tone rose higher than its own container. Not a disaster—an awakening. At the peak, the Prime Realm exceeded its limit and became many: Frequencies born as harmonics, each a clean note cast off from the original sound.

## Notes

Often paired with the belief that the Prime Realm could return if the Frequencies ever fall back into one shared harmony.
