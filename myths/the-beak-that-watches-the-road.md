# The Beak That Watches the Road

## Summary

A Hearthline warning myth says a giant blue bird marks disobedient wanderers and takes those who leave safe route rhythm.

## Myth Text

Parents tell children that the bird does not hunt hunger first; it hunts broken cadence. Those who answer late, stray wide, or mock route calls are said to draw the shadow before the beak. In gentler tellings, the warning is merciful: stay with your people and the sky will pass over you.

## Notes

The myth is widely used as child-safety language in high-risk travel zones.
