# The Flag That Never Docked

## Summary

A Driftfall myth says Vexra's flag was never meant to rest at any port.

## Myth Text

In the tale, each harbor that tried to claim Vexra's allegiance woke to empty moorings and a vanished crew by sunrise.  
The flag reappeared only where conflict and opportunity intersected.

To Driftfall loyalists, this proved that true command belongs to movement, not territory.

## Notes

Frequently cited as ideological justification for Vexra's fluid coalition model.
