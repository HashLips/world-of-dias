# The Golden Tears of Averra

## Summary

An Averra myth says Sael's golden tears appear when knowledge is about to vanish.

## Myth Text

The story claims Sael weeps gold only at moments where memory fractures widen and important truth risks being erased.  
Cloister keepers then seal archives, move artifacts, or alter access before outside hands arrive.

For believers, each tear is warning; for critics, each tear is performance used to justify concealment.

## Notes

Often cited in arguments over whether Sael preserves truth or curates power.
