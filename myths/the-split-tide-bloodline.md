# The Split Tide Bloodline

## Summary

A maritime myth claims Marek Solon's dual-form body is inherited fracture scarring from an older F432 break.

## Myth Text

Sea-elders say some bloodlines carried resonance damage after early fracture events, and that Marek is the clearest living example.  
In this telling, the body appears in two places not by magic trick, but by inherited split-tide memory written into form itself.

The story persists because Marek's condition has no widely accepted technical explanation.

## Notes

Often cited to support Marek's side mission of documenting pre-fracture remnants.
