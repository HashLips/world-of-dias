# The Day the Rain Learned Names

## Summary

An F380 myth claims there was one season when the rain fell neutrally until people began speaking one another's names with care, after which the estuary currents stabilized.

## Myth Text

The story's point is relational, not meteorological: unnamed crowds produced turbulence, while recognized persons produced steadier flow. Whether literal or symbolic, the tale is cited whenever communities need to rebuild trust.

## Notes

Commonly invoked by Tideheart facilitators during convergence rituals.
