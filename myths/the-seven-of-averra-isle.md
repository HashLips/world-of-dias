# The Seven of Averra Isle

## Summary

A persistent rumor across Nauw claims seven cloaked figures dwell on Averra Isle, acting as a hidden council that steers mysterious events and preserves a precarious balance between good and evil.

Their intentions are considered fundamentally unclear: if they exist, they may be benevolent, malevolent, or shifting between both.

## Myth Text

Dock workers whisper that no chart can keep Averra still: lights appear where no houses stand, bells ring in weather too calm for masts, and sometimes seven silhouettes are seen along the cliffs at dusk. In market retellings, these figures are called the Seven of Averra Isle. Some say they decide which fortunes rise and which break. Others say they do less and more than rule: they keep a balance no city council can see, nudging conflict away from collapse.

The boldest versions extend their reach beyond Nauw itself, claiming the Seven influence rare perfect-form fruits that appear in Wabet, especially in Verdant Reach. Believers call those appearances signs of judgment or favor; skeptics call them coincidence wrapped in fear.

This ambiguity is central to the myth's survival. Supporters point to unusually fortunate outcomes as evidence of protection, while detractors point to misfortune and disruption as evidence of manipulation.

## Notes

No verified expedition has confirmed the Seven's existence. The myth remains compatible with ongoing uncertainty around Averra Isle and other local lore traditions.
