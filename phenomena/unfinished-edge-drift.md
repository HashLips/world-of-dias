# Unfinished edge drift

## Name

Unfinished edge drift

## Overview

Unfinished edge drift is the recurring condition in which local boundaries, edges, and route contours in The Unfinished shift by small but meaningful increments without catastrophic terrain collapse.

## Description

The drift typically manifests as contour bleed, alignment marker migration, and minor relocation of path-ready surfaces. Structural cores usually persist; margins are what move. Because drift is incremental rather than violent, experienced travelers can compensate using multi-pass charts.

## Conditions

Most common near the Erasure Verge and after high activity periods in the Revision Delta. Reports suggest reduced drift where maintenance teams refresh guide marks on short cadence cycles.

## Cultural Interpretations

- F960 locals read drift as normal realm respiration, not disaster.
- Outsiders often misread it as corruption or map failure.
- Technical practitioners frame it as a maintenance problem: update often, travel intelligently.

## Notes

Drift creates plot pressure without needing apocalypse stakes.
