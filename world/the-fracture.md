# The Fracture

## Overview

The Fracture is the ancient event in which the Prime Realm shattered into the Frequencies.

## Description

During the Fracture, reality did not end—it separated into vibrational states of itself. Each resulting Frequency represents a different resonance of the original Prime Realm, and the laws of reality are not identical across all Frequencies as a result.

Because each fragment vibrates differently, different Frequencies may exhibit:

- different physical laws
- different gravity and energy behaviors
- different environments
- different lifeforms and inhabitants

## Lore

The time before the Fracture is commonly called **The Prime Age**, and the era after is commonly called **The Present Age**.

The cause of the Fracture remains unknown. Across the Frequencies, myths and theories diverge—some describing a natural cosmic limit, others a forgotten civilization, a betrayal, or a war that ruptured reality.

## Notes

The Fracture is treated as historical fact; its cause is not.
