# The Prime Age

## Overview

The Prime Age is the name given to the time before the Fracture, when the Prime Realm was whole.

## Description

Very little is known of this era. No complete records survive, and what knowledge exists comes from aligned ruins, repeating symbols, and objects that behave inconsistently with modern reality.

Remnants attributed to this era are commonly called **Prime Relics**.

## Lore

Explorers and scholars have noted that some ruins discovered across different Frequencies appear to align with one another when their locations are compared, suggesting they were once contiguous structures in the single unified Prime Realm.

One of the most discussed Prime Age remnants is **The Prime Bridge**, which persists as partial echoes across multiple Frequencies.

## Notes

“Prime Age” is a modern label for a period that left incomplete traces.
