# The Present Age

## Overview

The Present Age is the era after the Fracture, in which civilizations live across the Frequencies.

## Description

Most inhabitants accept the Frequencies as the natural structure of reality, though many still search for answers about the lost Prime Realm and the cause of the Fracture.

## Lore

Ruins, artifacts, myths, and unexplained phenomena continue to hint that reality was once unified. While modern inter-frequency travel exists in some form, it is generally understood to be unstable and incomplete compared to whatever principles governed the Prime Age.

## Notes

The shared scholarly consensus across many Frequencies is simple: the Frequencies were not always separate.
