# Dias

## Overview

Dias is the world in which all known existence unfolds.

## Core Premise

In this universe, reality is shaped not only by matter and time but also by **frequency**. Different layers of reality resonate at different frequencies, forming what are commonly called **frequency realms**. These realms coexist within the same cosmic space but normally remain separate from one another.

The exact number of frequencies that exist in Dias is unknown. Scholars, travelers, and explorers have only encountered a small portion of them. Each frequency may host its own landscapes, inhabitants, and laws of reality. Because of this structure, movement between realms is not necessarily a matter of distance but may involve changes in resonance or frequency.

## The Prime Realm and the Fracture

Before the Frequencies, there was only **The Prime Realm**—a single unified reality. At some unknown point in the distant past, an event now known as **The Fracture** shattered that unity, separating reality into vibrational states of itself. These states became the Frequencies.

No living being witnessed the Prime Realm, and no surviving record fully explains why the Fracture happened. The separation is treated as historical fact; the cause remains unknown.

The time before the Fracture is referred to as **The Prime Age**. The era after is commonly called **The Present Age**.

## Global Lore

Known frequency realms documented so far include F432; many others may exist uncharted.

Across the Frequencies, scattered ruins and **Prime Relics** hint that parts of reality once belonged to a single world. One of the most discussed remnants is **The Prime Bridge**, a fragmented structure believed to persist as partial echoes across multiple Frequencies.

Across different cultures and realms, many myths attempt to explain the Fracture. Some claim it was a natural cosmic event; others blame a forgotten civilization, betrayal, war, or a resonance threshold the Prime Realm could not contain.
