# Two Ways

## Overview

*Two Ways* is an artwork depiction of a Wabet fruit anomaly: ordinary organic fruit forms appearing alongside near-perfectly round forms that look almost artificial.

## Description

The piece focuses on an apple presentation to contrast two recurring appearances reported in Wabet and across F432 fruit circulation: naturally irregular fruit shapes and highly symmetric, near-perfect shapes. While apples are foregrounded, the same reported pattern extends to oranges, bananas, and other fruit varieties not yet formally classified.

Asset reference: `assets/2-ways.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual anchor for documenting fruit morphology variation in F432 before full botanical taxonomy is established.

## Lore Connection

In-world rumor calls the perfect-form variants **Lucky Fruits**. Popular belief holds that eating such fruit can produce unusually favorable outcomes, often described as fruit-specific:

- apple: exceptional luck
- banana: heightened intelligence
- orange: elevated vitality and recovery

No stable mechanism has been proven. However, repeated anecdotal patterns across markets and households keep the belief active, and some scholars classify Lucky Fruits as a possible low-frequency anomaly rather than pure superstition.

Within current trade lore, **Verdant Reach in Wabet** is cited as the most common source region where these near-perfect Lucky Fruits are found from time to time.

## Notes

Lucky Fruit effects remain culturally accepted but scientifically unresolved.
