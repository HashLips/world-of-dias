# Between Two Places

## Overview

*Between Two Places* is an artwork depicting the annual passage of the Mega Butterfly across Nauw, from its western birth zone near Averra Isle toward the eastern Glasswater Fields.

## Description

The piece centers on the contrast between ordinary butterflies and the Mega Butterfly: a colossal winged form that can appear almost unreal in scale. Its movement across the region is framed as both graceful and imposing, with implied risk to inhabitants below.

Asset reference: `assets/between-two-places.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual anchor for the Mega Butterfly cycle, giving a single recognizable image to a phenomenon mostly known through seasonal sightings and stories.

## Lore Connection

In-world readers associate the artwork with migration season in Nauw, when Mega Butterflies leave the Averra Isle zone and travel east to the clean waters of Glasswater Fields. The image is often discussed alongside rumors of inhabitants being lifted and carried by these creatures during transit.

## Notes

The artwork title reflects the species' long crossing between western origin and eastern resting grounds.
