# World of Dias Infographic

## Overview

*World of Dias Infographic* is a dense, council-style overview of **Dias** as a **stacked-frequency** cosmology: the **Fracture**, the **Prime Realm** as lost unity, **F432** as a central surveyed landmass (**Wabet**, **Nauw**, **Sorel**), sibling **frequency bands** (including F200 through F960) with short character lines, **peoples** and **survey** iconography, **ways of travel** and **crossing** methods, and **Prime Relics**—all in a single high-readability reference layout.

## Description

The piece presents **reality as layered resonance** rather than a single plane, with marginal **band status** and **ledger**-style notes that echo the **Supplemental Harmonic Ledger** tone used across the map series. The **F432** map reads as the current **go-to macro chart** for the stable realm; side and footer panels place that frame inside **Dias-wide** rules of access, bleed (lumen–abyss), and incomplete knowledge of other bands.

Asset reference: `assets/world-of-dias-info-graphic.png`.

## Real-World Role

Primary **current-state** explainer for collaborators and audiences: one image to orient to **Dias**, **F432** geography, and the **named frequency bands** without reading every regional file.

## Lore Connection

The themes align with **The Fracture**, **Prime Relics**, and the **frequency-realm** set already in text lore. It is a **presentation mirror** of that canon, not a replacement for detailed region entries.

## Notes

Marginal labels, exact **band status** gradations, and any **dated** survey lines are **artwork flavor** until mirrored in artifacts or stories. Cross-link the **F200–F960** and **F432** map artworks for band-level detail.
