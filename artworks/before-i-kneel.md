# Before I Kneel

## Overview

*Before I Kneel* depicts a contorted white figure braced against a black vertical line, staged as a threshold trial between submission and principle.

## Description

The body is folded into an almost impossible posture, signaling strain without collapse. Minimal color accents (blue marks at eye and limb) read as focal points of intent, while the severe red field suggests public scrutiny and consequence.

Asset reference: `assets/before-i-kneel.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual anchor for story beats about gate law, oath-taking, and principled resistance in Veloria.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the image is tied to the **Before-I-Kneel Oath**, recited at the DEB Gate when ward officers swear to uphold law without humiliating the lawful traveler.

## Notes

Often hung in training halls where gate discipline is taught as restraint as much as force.
