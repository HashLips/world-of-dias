# Hunger

## Overview

*Hunger* is an artwork by Daniel Eugene Botha that depicts hands reaching toward bananas, expressing Nauw's intense fruit desire and banana-centered food values.

## Description

The piece uses clustered banana forms and grasping hands to dramatize urgency, appetite, and competition around fruit access. The visual emphasis on bananas communicates their symbolic rank as the highest fruit in Nauw's food imagination.

Asset reference: `assets/hunger.png`.

## Real-World Role

A key interpretive image for understanding how food preference becomes identity inside Nauw.

## Lore Connection

In-world, *Hunger* reflects a region where fruit is culturally supreme but largely imported. It points to Nauw's dependence on Wabet for fruit supply and to the social intensity produced by that dependency.
