# Poster of Veloria City

## Overview

*Poster of Veloria City* is a promotional-style depiction of approach traffic entering Veloria.

## Description

The poster frames a cart of inhabitants traveling toward Veloria City's monumental gate and urban mass, emphasizing both human-scale movement and city-scale grandeur.

Asset reference: `assets/poster-of-veloria-city.PNG`.

## Real-World Role

A presentation image used to introduce Veloria City through an accessible arrival narrative.

## Lore Connection

The scene reflects established lore of Veloria as a major urban center that draws travelers, merchants, and cross-regional movement.

## Notes

Pairs well with city and gate entries when presenting public-facing world overviews.
