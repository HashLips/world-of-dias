# Seven in My Dreams Is

## Overview

*Seven in My Dreams Is* is an artwork depicting seven cloaked figures associated in local imagination with the myth of the Seven of Averra Isle.

## Description

The composition presents a dark, uneasy atmosphere in which the figures appear more sensed than fully known, reinforcing the impression of hidden authority and unresolved threat. Their grouping suggests deliberation or collective purpose rather than random presence.

Asset reference: `assets/7-in-my-dreams-is.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual anchor for one of Nauw's most persistent island rumors, used to communicate how fear, reverence, and uncertainty blend in Averra-related folklore.

## Lore Connection

In-world audiences often read the piece as a depiction of the rumored hidden council said to influence unexplained events across Nauw. Some interpreters connect the figures to stories that link Averra's unseen powers to the appearance of Lucky Fruits in Wabet, though such claims remain unverified.

## Notes

The artwork canonizes the image tradition around the rumor, not the factual existence of the Seven.
