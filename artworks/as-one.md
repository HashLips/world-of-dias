# As One

## Overview

*As One* depicts overlapping figures in a circular field, visually blending separate bodies into a single coordinated movement.

## Description

Translucent forms in red, blue-green, and neutral tones layer across each other, creating ambiguity between duet, struggle, and support. The circular backdrop reads as a shared boundary where individual stance and communal rhythm become inseparable.

Asset reference: `assets/as-one.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual reference for F432 scenes where collective timing, trust, and relational adaptation matter more than rank hierarchy.

## Lore Connection

In-world audiences often tie the piece to Velorian Basin social practice, especially the **Circle Step Accord** tradition where multi-race groups train coordination before negotiation.

## Notes

Used in story planning for moments when "agreement" is shown through movement rather than formal declaration.
