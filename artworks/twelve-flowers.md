# Twelve Flowers

## Overview

*Twelve Flowers* is an artwork depicting vibrant yellow flowers in a pot, reflecting a familiar streetscape motif found across Veloria City.

## Description

The composition emphasizes concentrated bloom color and container form, echoing the potted flower displays seen throughout Veloria's outer and inner rings. It presents floral presence not as rare decoration but as part of ordinary urban rhythm.

Asset reference: `assets/12-flowers.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual reference for Veloria's ring-spanning flower-vendor and flower-shop culture.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the image is read as representative of the flowers commonly sold in street markets and local shops across Veloria, where potted yellow arrangements are treated as both aesthetic expression and everyday civic ritual.

## Notes

The work is commonly paired with lore about ring-to-ring continuity in city street life.
