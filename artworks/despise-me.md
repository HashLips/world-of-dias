# Despise Me

## Overview

*Despise Me* portrays a dancer in a contorted, powerful pose, using body movement as both protest and celebration.

## Description

The figure's asymmetrical limbs and dynamic balance evoke performance at the edge of collapse and control. The composition suggests that joy can be an act of resistance under social judgment.

Asset reference: `assets/despise-me.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A character-energy artwork representing dance as a survival language in hard social environments.

## Lore Connection

In Sorel, the piece is linked to stories of a dancer who can move to any rhythm and shift a crowd's emotional state toward courage.

## Notes

Often used when discussing morale restoration after conflict cycles.
