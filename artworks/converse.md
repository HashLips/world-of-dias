# Converse

## Overview

*Converse* depicts a surreal field of white heads on elongated necks, clustered as if planted in shared ground while speaking, laughing, arguing, and whispering in continuous overlap.

## Description

The figures vary in height and posture, with some necklines extending over others as though trying to catch or redirect nearby voices. The scene feels playful and unsettling at once, presenting conversation as a living terrain.

Asset reference: `assets/convers.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A social-surreal anchor used to represent group mood, rumor cycles, and conversational pressure in Dias.

## Lore Connection

In Bloomline interpretations, the piece is associated with sites where collective emotion intensifies speech until distinctions between gossip, warning, and ritual exchange blur.
