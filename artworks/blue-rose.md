# Blue Rose

## Overview

*Blue Rose* shows a pale hand holding a drooping blue flower by a thin red stem, framing hope and sorrow as simultaneous states.

## Description

The image balances tenderness and decline: the flower bends, but is not dropped. The hand remains steady, suggesting commitment through emotional weight rather than denial of pain.

Asset reference: `assets/blue-rose.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A symbolic artwork used to communicate steadfast belief during periods of loss, uncertainty, or displacement.

## Lore Connection

In Sorel-aligned readings, the piece is linked to Blue Rose vow practice: keeping faith with unfinished futures even when present conditions deteriorate.
