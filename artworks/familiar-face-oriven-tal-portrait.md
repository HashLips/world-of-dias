# Familiar Face (Oriven Tal Portrait)

## Overview

*Familiar Face* depicts the elusive Ring Regent, Oriven Tal.

## Description

The subject appears recognizable but distant, preserving uncertainty about identity and presence.

The portrait is framed to feel known and unknown at once, reinforcing the tension between public title and private invisibility.

Asset reference: `assets/familiar-face.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A reference artwork for Oriven's hidden-authority character profile.

## Lore Connection

The piece supports stories where Oriven is known by name yet rarely witnessed directly.
Some readings interpret the image as evidence that "Oriven Tal" may function as a role-mask carried by hidden operators.

## Notes

Used as a primary visual cue for Veloria's secretive leadership layer.
