# 1 VEL Mark

## Overview

*1 VEL Mark* is the baseline note artwork in the VEL currency series used across F432.

## Description

The note is a long streamer-style rectangle with a dark blue and aged-gold palette, ringed border ornaments, and a large circular `V` medallion on the left. The central panel shows two birds crossing above a dense Veloria skyline and route markers for Wabet, Nauw, and Sorel, while the right value panel carries **ONE VEL**, **1 VEL MARK**, and a serial line. The composition reads as welcoming and civic: small-denomination trust anchored in city life and shared movement.

Asset reference: `assets/1-vel-mark.png`.

## Real-World Role

Primary visual reference for the smallest practical VEL denomination.

## Lore Connection

Represents street-level trade in Veloria and nearby Nauw routes while remaining recognizable as part of the broader F432 trade standard.

## Notes

Series typography and seal geometry are treated as artwork-defined until separately codified in an artifact or rule entry.
