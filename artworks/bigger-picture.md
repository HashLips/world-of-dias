# Bigger Picture

## Overview

*Bigger Picture* depicts a sparse room marked by blood-toned footprints that appear to move from interior floor to open window without showing the body that made them.

## Description

The composition uses absence as the subject. The only clear trail is the sequence of prints, climbing from grounded interior space toward an impossible exit line at the window frame. The effect is not gore-forward but evidentiary: a scene where movement is undeniable while identity remains hidden.

Asset reference: `assets/bigger-picture.jpg`.

A visual anchor for Dias themes of partial knowledge, unseen actors, and interpretation under pressure.
