# Awake 1

## Overview

*Awake 1* presents a white figure and inverted reflection cut by a deep violet wedge, often interpreted as the moment a hidden self becomes visible under reveal pressure.

## Description

Angular light fields, offset geometry, and a vertical signal line create a diagnostic mood rather than a natural landscape. The standing body and its inverted counterpart appear connected but out of phase, suggesting wakefulness as alignment between two partial truths.
