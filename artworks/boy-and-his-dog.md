# Boy and His Dog

## Overview

*Boy and His Dog* is an artwork depicting Andrew and Russell playing together on the outskirts of the Velorian Basin.

## Description

The scene captures a ball-throwing moment between the pair, framing the basin edge as a place of calm movement and familiar routine rather than commerce or tension.

Asset reference: `assets/boy-and-his-dog.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual reference for everyday life in Nauw beyond city-center systems and myth-heavy narratives.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the piece is treated as a snapshot of known local inhabitants whose presence is valued for warmth and spontaneity. It reinforces the idea that the social fabric of Velorian Basin includes simple recurring bonds alongside major trade and governance structures.

## Notes

Often cited as a gentle counterpoint to harsher frontier depictions elsewhere in F432.
