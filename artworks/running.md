# Running

## Overview

*Running* is an artwork by Daniel Eugene Botha associated with Harold's daily, multi-day circuit around Veloria City.

## Description

The piece presents a solitary runner in stylized motion, emphasizing speed, repetition, and forward intent. Its visual economy aligns with Harold's identity: movement as a discipline when all other anchors have fallen away.

Asset reference: `assets/running.gif`.

## Real-World Role

A compact visual expression of persistence and identity-through-action in Daniel Eugene Botha's wider body of world-linked artwork.

## Lore Connection

In-world, *Running* is read as Harold's ritual made visible: he runs to reclaim freedom and clarity, tracing Veloria's immense circumference over several days before beginning again.
