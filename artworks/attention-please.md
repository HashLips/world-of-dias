# Attention Please

## Overview

*Attention Please* is a still-life artwork depicting a bright patterned flower vessel with pale drooping flowers on a dark table, framed against split-toned walls.

## Description

The composition uses stark contrast: a vivid yellow-blue pot against shadow-heavy surroundings, with thin white stems bending outward as if reaching past the frame. Scattered white petals on the table imply recent bloom-loss, giving the piece a tension between decoration and decline.

Asset reference: `assets/attention-please.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual reference for F432 interior mood scenes where ordinary civic objects carry emotional or narrative weight.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the piece is commonly read as a Veloria Basin table study: a familiar street-flower vessel moved indoors, where it shifts from public ornament into a private signal that something in the room needs tending.

## Notes

Often paired with **Veloria Street Flower Pot** and **Twelve Flowers** as a tonal progression: civic bloom -> domestic bloom -> domestic warning.
