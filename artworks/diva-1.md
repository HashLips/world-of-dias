# Diva 1

## Overview

*Diva 1* is a close portrait of a luminous being with layered blue-violet chroma and a still, directive gaze.

## Description

The figure's face and upper body are rendered with distinct color planes that feel both biological and energetic. The expression is intensely focused, suggesting awareness that reads beyond ordinary social eye contact.
