# Name of It (Vexra Coil Portrait)

## Overview

*Name of It* is the principal portrait artwork linked to Vexra Coil.

## Description

A lone figure on a boat beneath a pirate-like flag captures Vexra's outlaw charisma and command-through-risk identity.

Asset reference: `assets/name-of-it.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

Primary visual for Driftfall's Free-Captain archetype.

## Lore Connection

Often cited in stories where Vexra's moving crews outpace fixed authority.

## Notes

The flag motif is interpreted as anti-hierarchy symbolism.
