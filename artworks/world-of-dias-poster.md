# World of Dias Poster

## Overview

*World of Dias Poster* is a **hero** presentation image for the setting: a **gold-titled** **World of Dias** over a **painterly** **harbor** and **tiered-city** vista, with **flat graphic overlays** and **F-band** callouts (F200, F380, F432, F500, F610, F840, F960) that read as **frequency emblems** on a single epic scene rather than a technical chart.

## Description

The composition mixes **realistic depth** (water, ships, crowding architecture, sky traffic) with **symbolic figures** and **realm tags**, selling the core line that **reality is not one layer** but **stacked frequency realms**. **F432** sits on the central city mass; other bands appear as **annotated focal points** around the frame, including ember-quiet and **unfinished** tonal hints at the lower range of the image.

Asset reference: `assets/world-of-dias-poster.png`.

## Real-World Role

Default **concept poster** for pitches, covers, and first-impression shares: mood and **Dias** identity before the infographic or map series.

## Lore Connection

Interprets **Nauw**-scale **crossroads** energy and **Dias**-scale **layering** in one image. It does not override the **F432 Map** line of assets for precise geography.

## Notes

Pairs with **World of Dias Infographic** for **mood-first** vs **reference-first** use. Overlay **F** labels and small scene illustrations are **artwork emphasis**; they support atmosphere and teaching, not strict in-world signage.
