# At Sea

## Overview

*At Sea* is an artwork depicting the Glasswater Field Drifters floating through the night on personal tubes across the southern dam waters of Glasswater Fields.

## Description

The image frames a calm, pure-water dam scene where multiple inhabitants drift in silence, each carried by a personal flotation tube they are known to keep for life. The mood is serene and contemplative, but the gathering is not leisure alone: it is a coordinated night watch focused on the sky and stars as evening sets in.

Asset reference: `assets/at-sea.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A core visual anchor for Glasswater Fields night-watch culture, establishing how vigilance and peace coexist in one shared ritual.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the piece is read as a truthful depiction of the region's evening sky-watch practice. Local communities respect the drifters because their overnight vigilance is treated as a protective duty for all settlements tied to the dams and nearby fields.

## Notes

The artwork emphasizes that stillness in Glasswater Fields can signal active guardianship rather than inactivity.
