# Bait

## Overview

*Bait* is an artwork set on Driftfall's eastern shores, depicting a lone inhabitant spending a casual beach day in a place everyone knows is unsafe for swimming.

## Description

The image shows a man hanging from what appears to be a beach line or clothesline while resting in the sun. Farther out, sharks are visible in the water and appear to be closing in. The scene captures a common Driftfall contradiction: people gather on the beach for leisure, but avoid entering the water because the shoreline is heavily shark-infested.

Asset reference: `assets/bait-1.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual anchor for Driftfall's eastern coastal mood, balancing humor, tension, and frontier nonchalance in a single still image.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the piece is read as a normal Driftfall moment rather than an extreme event. On Sorel's eastern side, beach activity is common: people hang on lines, throw frisbee, and idle in the sun while avoiding the water because of persistent shark danger. The image also reflects Driftfall's Baltic-leaning coastal mood, where edgy frontier energy and lazy, unhurried hanging-about coexist.

## Notes

The subject is treated as an unnamed Driftfall inhabitant passing time in the sun.
