# Two Phase (Vailor Quentin Portrait)

## Overview

*Two Phase* is a portrait artwork associated with Vailor Quentin's realm-level authority.

## Description

The figure appears pristine and controlled, wearing a red-sailed head symbol recognized across F432.

Composition emphasizes stillness and command: posture forward, expression restrained, and the sail form elevated as a realm signifier rather than decoration.

Asset reference: `assets/2-face.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual anchor for the High Resonant concept in leadership planning and story development.

## Lore Connection

In-world readings connect the piece to debates about Vailor's legitimacy, dominance, and symbolic rule.
It is often referenced in discussions of whether the red-sail emblem was taken in force or granted in recognition.

## Notes

The image is used as a leadership reference depiction.
