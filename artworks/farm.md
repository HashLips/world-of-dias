# Farm

## Overview

*Farm* is an artwork depicting the daily labor ethos of Verdant Farmers in Verdant Reach.

## Description

The piece frames farming as repetitive but dignified work: cultivation, care, and yield management carried out to sustain families and keep regional supply steady. Its visual tone emphasizes grounded responsibility over spectacle.

Asset reference: `assets/farm.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A reference image for the social identity of Verdant Reach's providers and the disciplined culture attached to long-cycle cultivation.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the artwork is commonly read as a portrait of people whose mission is not conquest or prestige, but continuity: maintaining fruit, grain, and other crop output for both household life and inter-regional flow across F432.

## Notes

Often paired conceptually with depictions of Lucky Farmers to contrast stewardship and opportunism.
