# Golden Teer (Sael Corvin Portrait)

## Overview

*Golden Teer* is the primary portrait artwork associated with Sael Corvin.

## Description

Sael appears vaguely and at distance, bearded and subdued, with visible golden tears that dominate interpretation of the image.

Asset reference: `assets/golden-teer.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

Visual anchor for Sael's memory-curation and fracture-signal lore.

## Lore Connection

Commonly linked to stories where golden tears indicate hidden truth under threat.
