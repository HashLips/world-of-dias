# Exhibition 1

## Overview

*Exhibition 1* depicts a bodiless blue-headed resident paused in a gallery corner, quietly facing a framed work under angled light.

## Description

The scene emphasizes stillness and looking. The resident appears physically simple but emotionally absorbed, suggesting that long-form observation is itself a form of participation.

Asset reference: `assets/exhibition-1.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A gallery-life artwork that centers the viewer, not only the viewed object.

## Lore Connection

In Sketchy Gallery tradition, the image is associated with residents who spend entire cycles watching rotating works without speaking, then leave at closing with visible calm.

## Notes

Commonly referenced in curation debates about attention as civic contribution.
