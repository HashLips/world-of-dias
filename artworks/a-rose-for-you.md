# A Rose for You

## Overview

*A Rose for You* is an artwork depicting a calm moonlit night over a rose-covered field in Wabet.

## Description

The scene is set on the rim where Verdant Reach meets Lumira Sands, presenting a serene transition zone between fertile growth and quiet desert edge. A moonlit backdrop and dense rose coverage emphasize stillness rather than drama.

Asset reference: `assets/a-rose-for-you.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual reference for Wabet's softer nighttime landscapes and the tonal contrast between productive farmland and tranquil boundary terrain.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the piece is read as evidence that the Verdant Reach-Lumira Sands boundary is not only an ecological edge but also a place of unusual calm, where floral abundance and desert silence briefly coexist.

## Notes

Often discussed as one of the most peaceful nocturnal depictions tied to Wabet.
