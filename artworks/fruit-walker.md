# Fruit Walker

## Overview

*Fruit Walker* is an artwork depiction of the rumored elder who appears across F432 carrying baskets of fruit and offering Lucky Fruit at selected doors.

## Description

The image presents an elderly traveler whose basketed cargo and unhurried posture frame him as both vendor and omen-bearing wanderer. The visual language emphasizes ambiguity: he reads as fully human, yet strangely untethered to normal route timing.

Asset reference: `assets/apple-slice.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A key visual anchor for one of F432's most persistent folklore figures, used to ground oral accounts in a consistent character image.

## Lore Connection

In-world readings tie the figure to Wabet-to-Nauw fruit movement and to household stories that his appearance carries special meaning. The piece does not settle whether the Fruit Walker is historically verifiable; it preserves the tension between rumor, myth, and testimony.

## Notes

The artwork depicts a disputed inhabitant profile rather than confirming literal historical certainty.
