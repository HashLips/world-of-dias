# Curious 1

## Overview

*Curious 1* presents a narrow rising pillar that tapers to a point, capped by an angled arch and a hooked curve ending in a small floating sphere.

## Description

Its minimal geometry reads as intentional ritual architecture rather than decorative abstraction. The vertical form implies ascent and focus, while the top arc and sphere suggest guidance, inquiry, and directional watch.

Asset reference: `assets/curious-1.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A symbolic-architectural artwork used to represent sacred wayfinding in Dias.

## Lore Connection

In Returning Span readings, this image is tied to the Curious Arch, a threshold emblem used to orient travelers before lane commitment and crossing.

## Notes

Commonly cited in discussions of discipline before movement.
