# Map of Sorel

## Overview

*Map of Sorel* is a close-up cartographic representation focused on Sorel within F432.

## Description

The map gives a clearer visual split between Hearthvale and Driftfall and emphasizes Sorel's bridge-linked relationship to southern Nauw.

Asset reference: `assets/map-of-sorel.PNG`.

## Real-World Role

A regional map used to present Sorel's internal territorial contrast at readable scale.

## Lore Connection

In canon framing, this is a clarity pass for established Sorel geography and not a reset of regional boundaries.
