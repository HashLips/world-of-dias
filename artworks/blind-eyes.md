# Blind Eyes

## Overview

*Blind Eyes* focuses on two enlarged eyes wrapped in layered veils, set against a warm field with sparse facial context.

## Description

The composition foregrounds seeing while obscuring the seer: lids are sharpened, pupils alert, but lower structures are bound by color bands that imply restraint, injury, or ritual masking. The doubled eye motif suggests paired testimonies that agree on form but not meaning.

Asset reference: `assets/blind-eyes.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual reference for stories where knowledge is partial, filtered, or ethically withheld.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the image is linked to Averra's **Blind Eyes Vigil**, where witnesses train to report what they can verify while refusing embellishment under pressure.

## Notes

Commonly paired with Sael Corvin narratives when discussing the line between concealment and care.
