# Senses 1 (Iskar Lüm Portrait)

## Overview

*Senses 1* is the principal artwork depiction of Iskar Lüm.

## Description

The figure appears as drifting body-signs rather than fixed anatomy, presenting eyes, mouth, and limb-like forms in unstable spatial relation.

Asset reference: `assets/senses-1.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

Primary visual reference for Iskar's non-human leadership form.

## Lore Connection

Closely tied to narratives about silence-law and hidden-water custodianship in Lumira Sands.

## Notes

Used whenever Iskar's otherworldly morphology is discussed.
