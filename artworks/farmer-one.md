# Farmer One

## Overview

*Farmer One* is an artwork depiction of the roaming Lucky Farmer archetype associated with Aelwyn Canopy.

## Description

The image language suggests a field-surveyor lifestyle: mobility, watchfulness, and opportunistic harvest timing rather than fixed settlement cultivation. It evokes the idea of daily "treasure-hunt" scouting for rare perfect-form Lucky Fruits.

Asset reference: `assets/farmer-1.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual reference for understanding the social type that emerged around Lucky Fruit rumor economies in northern Wabet.

## Lore Connection

In-world readings connect this figure to canopy camps and repeated survey routes through Aelwyn territory. It is often discussed alongside reports that Lucky Farmers are kept outside Verdant Reach's core growing zones by local farming communities.

## Notes

The artwork depicts a recognized social role, not proof of any specific Lucky Fruit effect.
