# Breakfast 1

## Overview

*Breakfast 1* is a sparse table composition centered on a mostly empty plate, interpreted as a household ritual scene in Verdant Reach.

## Description

Bright, flat color fields frame a near-minimal meal: one plated portion, scattered egg forms, and multiple chairs waiting around a pink tablecloth. The imbalance between seating and serving turns the image into a narrative prompt about expectation, delay, or remembered company.

Asset reference: `assets/breakfast-1.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A visual reference for intimate, low-stakes story beats where communal care is expressed through food and timing.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the piece is linked to **The Third-Chair Breakfast** custom in Verdant Reach, where one chair is intentionally left open for a late worker, returning traveler, or unresolved reconciliation.
