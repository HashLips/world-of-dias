# Green 1 (Serik Dovant Portrait)

## Overview

Portrait reference for Serik Dovant's role as High Steward of Nauw.

## Description

The image emphasizes controlled presence and practical authority.

Its visual language is restrained and deliberate, mirroring Serik's reputation for solving conflict through patient structure.

Asset reference: `assets/green-1.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

Leadership concept art for Nauw's central steward figure.

## Lore Connection

Associated with route balance and civic continuity in Nauw.

The portrait is often paired with the myth of the "crossing tables," where rival factions were said to sign peace terms under Serik's watch.

## Notes

Used as the core visual reference for Serik.
