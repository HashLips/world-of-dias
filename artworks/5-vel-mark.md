# 5 VEL Mark

## Overview

*5 VEL Mark* is a low-denomination note artwork in the VEL series, positioned above the base note for common market exchanges.

## Description

The elongated strip keeps the same left `V` medallion and circular border rhythm, but the center scene becomes busier: cranes, ships, dock edges, and mixed inhabitants in active exchange. Small flying forms and layered harbor silhouettes push the note toward movement and negotiation, while the right denomination box presents **FIVE VELLMARKS** and **5 VEL MARKS** with serial detail. The artwork reads as low-tier but high-traffic port commerce.

Asset reference: `assets/5-vel-mark.png`.

## Real-World Role

Visual anchor for the "small but meaningful" denomination used in routine cargo, stall, and transit-scale transactions.

## Lore Connection

Reinforces VEL as everyday inter-regional money in F432 rather than a niche city token.

## Notes

As with other denominations, decorative motifs are illustrative framing unless promoted into dedicated symbol or artifact lore.
