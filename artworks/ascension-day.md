# Ascension Day

## Overview

*Ascension Day* portrays two towering pale figures above a gathered crowd, one smiling and one stern, beneath a band-like sky form.

## Description

The composition stages power as spectacle and ambiguity at once: elevated forms dominate the frame while silhouetted witnesses remain grounded and indistinct. The dual expressions suggest competing readings of the same event - blessing or warning, elevation or separation.

Asset reference: `assets/ascension-day.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A key visual reference for ceremonial scenes involving public witness, symbolic authority, and divided interpretation in F432 lore.

## Lore Connection

In-world, the piece is commonly linked to Averra stories of the **Two Pillars Witness**, and to broader rumors that unseen councils shape outcomes through staged appearances.

## Notes

Frequently cited alongside **The Seven of Averra Isle** when discussing whether ritual events are civic necessity, manipulation, or both.
