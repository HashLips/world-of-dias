# Wall of Eyes

## Overview

*Wall of Eyes* portrays a massive purple wall covered in repeating gold-irised eyes that seem to track passersby without speaking.

## Description

Layered eye bands create an oppressive sense of collective observation. The eyes are not animated in the image, but the arrangement implies active follow-through as a viewer moves along the wall.

Asset reference: `assets/wall-of-eyes.jpg`.

## Real-World Role

A key visual for Dias themes of being watched, interpreted, and measured without direct confrontation.

## Lore Connection

In Glassfold circles, the piece is associated with wall-eye events where reflective surfaces appear to fix attention on specific travelers during unstable intervals.

## Notes

The artwork is commonly used in testimony training examples about perceived scrutiny under stress.
