# Brindle March

## Overview

Brindle March is Nauw's central belt of roads and bridges, running between the Velorian Basin (north-west, around Veloria City) and the Glasswater Fields to the east. It also links Nauw to neighboring realms.

## Description

Geographically, the march sits in the middle of Nauw: westward and north-westward connections feed the basin and the capital; eastward lanes open onto the great agricultural spread of Glasswater Fields. The network is composed of interconnected roads, bridge systems, and traveling settlements that support constant movement.

It remains the primary terrestrial path from central Nauw to Wabet and, along its southern reach, toward Sorel. The southern reach feeds the great span that crosses from southern Nauw into Hearthvale on Sorel's northern side, making Brindle March the usual overland approach for that crossing.

Many distant travelers first encounter Nauw through Brindle March before moving toward Veloria City and the basin markets.

## Lore

The guiding law of Brindle March is: "Every traveler has a road."

Travelers repeat a rumor that the resilient charms on certain old spans and toll-wards along the march trace back to students of the Old Masters on Averra Isle. The tale is widely told and weakly documented; see Old Masters of Averra Isle.
