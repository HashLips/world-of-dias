# Glasswater Fields

## Overview

Glasswater Fields is Nauw's eastern agricultural heartland—open country of reflective rivers, farms, and windmill landscapes that occupies most of the region's eastern half.

## Description

The territory lies almost entirely to the east of central Nauw, with Brindle March and the Velorian Basin to the west carrying its harvests toward Veloria City and wider trade. The waters are known for mirror-like clarity, often reflecting the sky with near-glass precision. Across these fields, farm communities and windmill clusters maintain grain and livestock production at scales that sustain urban demand.

Glasswater Fields underpins food reliability for Veloria and surrounding basin towns.

## Lore

The guiding law of Glasswater Fields is: "Stability feeds the many."

Folk tradition—not treated as fact by scholars—holds that much of the useful magic still worked in these fields (blessings on storage, water-warding, calm weather for harvest) descends from teachings first given on Averra Isle by the so-called Old Masters. See Old Masters of Averra Isle.
