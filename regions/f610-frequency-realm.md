# F610 (frequency realm)

## Overview

**F610** is called the **Reveal Veil**: a frequency characterized by short-lived windows where concealed structure becomes visible.

## Description

F610 is neither a lie-detector world nor omniscient space. Its defining behavior is interval-based disclosure: pattern, relation, and underlying form become legible briefly, then return behind ordinary opacity.

Cartographic depictions favor crystalline folds, mirrored ledges, and thin transparency bands with timed annotation marks.

## Lore

The key internal region is **The Glassfold Ledge**, where reveal intervals are most reliable and studied.

### Awareness in F432

In F432, F610 is mostly referenced in investigative circles and cautionary myths: "you may see truly, but not for long."

## Stories or Depictions

Art and diagrams represent F610 with layered panes, split reflections, and brief alignment notches rather than broad territorial borders.

The **F610 Map** artwork is the current visual reference for **The Glassfold Ledge** and foldline / window-shelf navigation; see `assets/f610-map.png`.

## Notes

Use F610 to support mystery and revelation pacing, especially in stories where truth arrives in limited windows.
