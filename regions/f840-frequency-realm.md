# F840 (frequency realm)

## Overview

**F840** is known as the **Ember Quiet**: a recovery frequency where the dominant condition is not catastrophe, but what remains and what can be rebuilt.

## Description

F840 reads as post-event continuity. Surfaces hold trace heat, skies stay muted, and ecosystems favor hardy regrowth over dramatic bloom. The realm feels contemplative without being dead.

Maps use ash-ember gradients, hearthline markers, and low-intensity flow paths indicating where warmth and livability persist.

## Lore

The principal internal region is **The Ashen Hearthline**, a mapped corridor of stable warmth, shelter craft, and long-term restoration practice.

### Awareness in F432

F432 storytellers invoke F840 as proof that endings and injuries are not the final phase of a worldline. Official institutions mention it rarely but respectfully.

## Stories or Depictions

F840 depictions emphasize sheltered routes, durable materials, and quiet communal continuity rather than conflict fronts.

The **F840 Map** artwork is the current visual reference for **The Ashen Hearthline** and ember/relay routing; see `assets/f840-map.png`.

## Notes

Use F840 to enrich healing arcs and post-crisis chapters without collapsing tone into despair.
