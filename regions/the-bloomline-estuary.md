# The Bloomline Estuary

## Overview

The Bloomline Estuary is F380's map anchor: a confluence region where emotional-state currents meet and separate in trackable channels.

## Description

**Map portrayal guidance**  
Represent as a fan-shaped estuary with color-gradient tributaries instead of conventional water-only channels. Keep five major "mood tributaries" flowing into one shared basin:

- Warm Current (amber-rose)
- Clear Current (silver-blue)
- Heavy Current (slate-violet)
- Bright Current (gold-green)
- Quiet Current (smoke-teal)

Boundaries should blur at edges and sharpen near convergence nodes. Label major confluence points as "holding coves" where groups stabilize local tone for gathering, healing, or negotiation.

## Lore

Skilled guides track not only direction and depth but affective pressure. Settlements form near predictable convergence zones where volatility is manageable.

## Stories or Depictions

Maps often annotate the same path with two recommended timings: "clear-day" and "heavy-day" transit.

## Notes

Render as living climate cartography, not weather fantasy spectacle.
