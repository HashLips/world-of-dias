# Lumira Sands

## Overview

Lumira Sands is the southern desert region of Wabet, known for pale dunes, hidden oases, and an unusual atmosphere of calm.

## Description

Although outwardly arid, Lumira Sands contains underground water flows that feed concealed oasis pockets and sustain rare plant and animal life. The terrain extends in long dune fields with sparse but resilient ecological nodes.

Travelers across Dias often describe Lumira Sands as unusually quiet and balanced compared to harsher desert zones elsewhere.

Its domain covers the lower portion of Wabet, anchoring the south beneath Verdant Reach.

## Lore

The guiding law of Lumira Sands is: "The land provides for those who respect its silence."
