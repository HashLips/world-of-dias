# Velorian Basin

## Overview

Velorian Basin is the valley and low country that rings Veloria City in the north-west of Nauw. It is one of the busiest exchange zones in F432.

## Description

The basin encircles the capital rather than sitting at Nauw's geographic center. Caravan roads, river routes, and market towns spread outward from Veloria across the bowl of the land, creating dense movement corridors between the city and its outer basin towns.

The region functions as a logistical center where goods, labor, and travelers converge continuously. Its activity level makes it one of the most consistently populated and commercially active landscapes in the realm. To the south-east, travel corridors connect toward central Nauw and Brindle March; farther east lie the Glasswater Fields.

## Lore

The guiding law of Velorian Basin is: "Exchange creates connection."

Market lore and street parables sometimes claim that the first contract-magic and luck-cantrips used in basin trade were carried from Averra Isle by those who studied under the Old Masters. The story remains myth, not institutional history; see Old Masters of Averra Isle.
