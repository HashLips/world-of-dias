# F200 (frequency realm)

## Overview

F200 is a high-resonance frequency realm within Dias, commonly described as **pure luminosity**: an atmosphere of calm, golden light where matter reads less like soil and water and more like **held radiance** and stable harmonic fields.

## Description

**Cartographic / map portrayal (for visualization)**  
When F200 is drawn as a map, the realm is most often shown as a **very long, narrow rectangle**—width modest relative to length, edges ruler-clean—oriented as if it were a **single luminous band** cut from a spectrum. The fill is not terrain in the usual sense: there is **no greenery, no blue hydrology, no weathered rock**. Instead, the “ground” and “sky” read as a continuous field of **warm gold-to-champagne light**, with subtle shifts in **brightness, grain, and striation** where older charts imply stable harmonic zones. Fine geometric seams—hairline parallax lines, barely visible as lighter filaments—occasionally suggest fold-planes, like light folded on itself.

**Atmosphere and scale**  
The air, if the word applies, feels **serene, weightless, and intensely alive without aggression**. Sound tends to be soft, bell-like, or absent; color temperature skews **late-afternoon sun through pale amber glass**. Distant “depth” is often represented as a gentle falloff from brighter foreground panels to a slightly dimmer, more diffuse distance—never storm, never night.

**Life**  
F200 supports **energy-native beings** and structures that look like **architecture made of filaments, lenses, and standing waves** rather than cut stone. Carbon-style ecosystems as known from F432 do not map cleanly here: ecologies are **light-flow ecologies**—communities and phenomena organized around current, coherence, and pattern preservation.

## Lore

The best-documented **inhabited structure** within F200 is **Aurel Meridian**, a sub-region that appears on charts as a **second long rectangle** nested inside the realm’s overall rectangle—an energetic corridor or “bright spine” where energy-kind populations and stable phenomena are most often reported.

F200 stands in **sharp contrast** to the low-resonance **F500** abyss: where F200 reads as **clarity, saturation, and composed peace**, F500 reads as **absence, inversion, and unbounded dark**.

### Awareness in F432

F432 does not “see” F200 the way it sees a coastline. Knowledge arrives as **partial harmonics**: **leaked glow in the sky**, instrument jokes, and **disputed index fragments**—enough to **name** the band and guess at **serene energy life**, not enough for most ring-cities to treat it as a **trade partner** or **mapped ally**. Encounters matter for **story and omen**, rarely for **day-to-day governance** in Nauw, Wabet, or Sorel.

## Stories or Depictions

Travelers’ tales from other frequencies often flatten F200 into a **single geometric icon** (triangle, circle, or line) in marginal diagrams—simplified emblems of **high purity and order**—while actual realm maps return to the **elongated rectangle** as the more faithful large-scale frame.

The **F200 Map** artwork is the current **visual reference** for that large-scale frame, **Aurel Meridian** as the bright spine, and **Dias**-level context panels; see `assets/f200-map.png` and the artwork entry **F200 Map**.

## Notes

F200 and F432 are both frequency realms, but F200’s **physics-of-appearance** is less material and more **luminous-harmonic**; do not assume F432 cartographic conventions (forests, seas, frontiers) transfer without translation.
