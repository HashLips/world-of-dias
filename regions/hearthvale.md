# Hearthvale

## Overview

Hearthvale is the western territory of Sorel, centered on rebuilding stable life after exile. It receives many who leave Nauw under pressure of shame, law, or custom.

## Description

Hearthvale covers the western half of Sorel. The territory is characterized by small towns, cooperative farms, and shared workshops designed for long-term continuity rather than temporary encampment. Communities emphasize mutual support and practical restoration.

Residents often frame Hearthvale as proof that displacement does not end a person's social future.

A major bridge runs from southern Nauw to Sorel's northern frontier within Hearthvale, making it the principal crossing for people and goods from Nauw into northern Sorel.

## Lore

The guiding law of Hearthvale is: "Every person deserves a second beginning."
