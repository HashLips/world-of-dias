# The Ashen Hearthline

## Overview

The Ashen Hearthline is F840's map core: a long corridor network of stable warmth and viable shelter routes across muted post-event terrain.

## Description

**Map portrayal guidance**  
Map as a branching hearth-corridor spine running through ash plains and low ember ridges. Include:

- **Core Hearth Spine**: the most reliable warm route; dotted with shelter stations.
- **Kindle Spurs**: secondary branches with variable heat stability.
- **Cold Gaps**: traversable intervals requiring timing, gear, or guided passage.

Use dark ashen base tones with subtle ember veins and orange-gold nodes at shelter points. Emphasize survivability networks over dramatic topography.

## Lore

Communities cluster around persistent heat nodes and maintain relay chains for travelers, supplies, and message continuity.

## Stories or Depictions

Hearthline maps are often marked with "rest viability" ratings rather than conventional distance-first legends.

## Notes

Render as durable, humane infrastructure in a quiet realm, not as scorched wasteland spectacle.
