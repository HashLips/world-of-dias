# DEB Gate of Veloria

## Overview

The DEB Gate of Veloria is the principal entrance landmark to Veloria City: a massive dual-gate structure whose silhouette echoes an upside-down D and E form, based on the DEB logo shape.

## Description

The entrance complex rises to the height of the highest fourth-layer towers and stands within the fortified boundary of the city. Veloria is enclosed by an enormous sandstone wall, and this gate system is built into that defensive perimeter.

The gateway consists of two monumental gate leaves, normally kept shut and opened only when visitors are permitted to enter the city. This controlled opening is intended to safeguard Veloria's residents and maintain order at the city's threshold.

Materially, the structure combines stone, gold, silver, and wood: major framing and wall integration in stone; gateworks in gold and silver; and large wooden doors reinforced with silver ornamentation. Engraved across the doors are floating figures representing the Sky Drifters.

## Lore

In local interpretation, the gate serves both as a defense mechanism and as a statement of identity: Veloria's boundary is physically sealed, but visually tied to symbolic forms and skyborne mysteries.
