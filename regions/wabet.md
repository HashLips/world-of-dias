# Wabet

## Overview

Wabet occupies the western lands of the F432 frequency realm, spanning the full western side of the regional map.

## Description

Wabet stretches along the entire western boundary of F432. It borders Nauw to the east and Sorel to the southeast. Unlike other regions of F432, Wabet is widely known as a place of natural purity and quiet serenity.

Its landscapes include vast forests, untouched jungles, clear rivers, deserts, and scattered chains of small islands along its coasts. The waters are said to be exceptionally pure, and many travelers describe the air itself as unusually clear and calm.

Human influence in Wabet is minimal. No dominant culture or governing authority controls the region, and settlements are sparse compared to other lands in F432. The few inhabitants who live there are scattered across the wilderness and often live in harmony with the surrounding environment.

Because of its untouched landscapes and peaceful atmosphere, travelers sometimes refer to Wabet as a place of cleansing or quiet reflection within the frequency realm.

## Lore

Wabet defines the western edge of F432 and stands in contrast to the heavily populated routes of Nauw and the harsh frontier character of Sorel. It is also a major fruit source for Nauw, whose markets and food culture depend on produce routes moving eastward from Wabet.

A recurring produce-side phenomenon is the appearance of near-perfect fruit forms among otherwise organic growth patterns. Traders and households often classify these as Lucky Fruits; while unproven in strict scientific terms, repeated anecdotal outcomes keep the belief active across F432.

Recognized subregions include Aelwyn Canopy across the north (with limited extension southward), Verdant Reach at the center, and Lumira Sands across the south. The major offshore island Isle of Calareth lies off Wabet's eastern side.
