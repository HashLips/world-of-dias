# F380 emotional climate coupling rule

## Name

F380 emotional climate coupling rule

## Overview

In F380, collective emotional state and local environmental expression are dynamically linked.

## Description

Large-group affect shifts can alter color fields, sound texture, and spatial feel over time. Effects are usually gradual and distributed, not instant spell-like transformations.

## Effect

- Makes social process a direct environmental factor.
- Elevates the civic role of attunement specialists.

## Notes

Single individuals can influence local tone, but stable change is primarily communal.
