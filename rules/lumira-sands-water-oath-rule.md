# Lumira Sands water oath rule

## Name

Lumira Sands water oath rule

## Overview

Mapped water points in Lumira are treated as shared survival infrastructure, not private leverage.

## Description

Guides and keepers may control route disclosure timing, but verified potable nodes cannot be sabotaged, concealed for harm, or sold as death-risk traps.

## Effect

- Sustains difficult coexistence among rival groups.
- Makes oath-breaking one of the most severe social offenses in the sands.

## Notes

Enforcement is mostly reputational, but the reputational penalty is effectively existential.
