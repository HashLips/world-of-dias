# Averra offshore courtesy rule

## Name

Averra offshore courtesy rule

## Overview

Mainland communities may answer Averra rituals with light or silence, but may not force contact at ceremonial hours.

## Description

During recognized offshore-pass windows, vessels hold distance and observers avoid signal escalation. Interpretive claims are permitted; intrusive actions are not.

## Effect

- Preserves ritual coexistence without political merger.
- Limits provocation events around high-rumor nights.

## Notes

Widely followed in practice even by people who reject Averra myths.
