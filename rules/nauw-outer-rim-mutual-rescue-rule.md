# Nauw Outer Rim mutual rescue rule

## Name

Nauw Outer Rim mutual rescue rule

## Overview

Any vessel capable of safe intervention must answer distress within outer-rim range.

## Description

Commercial priority, rival affiliation, and debt status do not void rescue obligation. Salvage claims may be argued later; life support cannot be delayed for contract reasons.

## Effect

- Maintains minimum trust in high-risk waters.
- Reduces retaliatory refusal cycles between competing crews.

## Notes

Violation marks a captain as unreliable across multiple ports, often permanently.
