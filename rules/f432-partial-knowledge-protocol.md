# F432 partial knowledge protocol

## Name

F432 partial knowledge protocol

## Overview

F432 institutions treat cross-frequency claims as usable only when tagged by confidence and source type.

## Description

Policy, trade, and safety decisions may reference rumors, but records must clearly separate witnessed fact, copied claim, and symbolic interpretation. Unknowns are not deleted; they are marked and carried forward.

## Effect

- Keeps governance functional despite incomplete cosmic knowledge.
- Prevents rumor from becoming law without explicit elevation.

## Notes

This rule explains why F432 can expand lore quickly without collapsing canon coherence.
