# Hearthvale shelter-first rule

## Name

Hearthvale shelter-first rule

## Overview

Immediate shelter and stabilization are prioritized before status judgment in Hearthvale.

## Description

New arrivals receive baseline protection, heat, and intake support before adjudication of dispute history. Accountability steps follow after stabilization, not before.

## Effect

- Cuts mortality and revenge-entry cycles.
- Attracts criticism from factions that prefer exclusion-first security models.

## Notes

This rule is one of the clearest practical signatures of Sorel's second-beginning ethos.
