# Bren Holloway

## Overview

Bren Holloway is the Road Marshal of Brindle March.

## Description

F432 race record: **Human**.

Bren is known for restoring movement under extreme conditions and treating transport continuity as regional lifeblood.

His head is permanently enclosed in a translucent gel-cube structure, an identifying feature that made him legendary across march routes.

## Beliefs About Them

Travel crews trust Bren's reliability under pressure. Some local enclaves resent his tendency to prioritize corridor flow over local autonomy.

## Narrative Role

Bren represents infrastructural heroism: leadership defined by keeping systems alive during crisis.

## Notes

His command legitimacy is strongest at bridges, spans, and contested crossings.
