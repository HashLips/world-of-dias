# Palewind Swarms

## Overview

Palewind Swarms are a dominant F432 race whose personhood is distributed across many micro-bodies acting as one coherent identity.

## Description

A single Palewind individual may appear as a drifting veil of motes, filament clusters, or temporary gathered forms. They can partition for short tasks and reconverge without losing continuity of self.

At rest, many appear as hovering clouds of pale particles with subtle internal currents; in social contexts, they often condense into rough humanoid or totemic silhouettes to ease interaction. Their visible "body" can thicken, thin, or branch as attention and intent shift.

## Beliefs About Them

Non-swarm societies sometimes mistake Palewind coordination for anonymity; Palewind legal traditions are explicit that each swarm identity is a distinct person with accountable continuity.

## Narrative Role

Palewind Swarms support stories about collective agency, memory distribution, and non-singular embodiment in public life.

## Notes

Partitioned units are functional extensions, not separate citizens by default unless a lineage-specific split rite says otherwise.
