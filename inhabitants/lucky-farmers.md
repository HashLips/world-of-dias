# Lucky Farmers

## Overview

Lucky Farmers are roaming harvesters associated with Aelwyn Canopy, known for searching specifically for rare perfect-form Lucky Fruits.

## Description

Unlike the broad cultivation discipline of Verdant Reach, Lucky Farmers operate more like trackers and prospectors. They camp, survey, and move repeatedly through Aelwyn zones, treating each day as a search for high-value fruit anomalies rather than routine crop management.

Their nickname reflects this narrow focus: they are seen less as general farmers and more as Lucky Fruit vendors and hunters.

## Beliefs About Them

Some communities admire their risk tolerance and field knowledge. Others view them as destabilizing opportunists whose methods prioritize rare finds over ecological continuity.

A recurring local account holds that Lucky Farmers are not welcome inside Verdant Reach's managed core zones, where Verdant Farmers enforce stricter cultivation boundaries.

## Narrative Role

They embody the speculative edge of Wabet's fruit economy: hope, rumor, and profit-seeking clustered around rare anomalies.

## Notes

Their reports are a major source of Lucky Fruit stories, but verification remains uneven.
