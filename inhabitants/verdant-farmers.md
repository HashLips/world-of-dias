# Verdant Farmers

## Overview

Verdant Farmers are the primary agricultural stewards of Verdant Reach in Wabet, responsible for sustaining stable fruit, grain, and crop supply chains across F432.

## Description

Their work centers on selecting, growing, nurturing, and protecting orchards and fields in one of the richest cultivation zones in the realm. They are known for discipline, routine, and practical long-term planning rather than rapid extraction.

The community ethos is straightforward: keep households fed, keep trade flowing, and preserve enough continuity for the next season. In local narratives, they are often described as humble, family-rooted people who measure success through stability rather than status.

## Beliefs About Them

Across nearby regions, Verdant Farmers are regarded as reliable providers whose labor quietly underwrites daily life beyond Verdant Reach itself.

## Narrative Role

They personify the everyday backbone of F432: patient agricultural work that sustains both local families and wider regional interdependence.

## Notes

They are widely described as protective of Verdant Reach's fields and cautious toward opportunistic harvest groups.
