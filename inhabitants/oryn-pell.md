# Oryn Pell

## Overview

Oryn Pell is the Canopy Elder of Aelwyn Canopy.

## Description

Trained in elevated walk settlements, Oryn rose through conflict mediation over upper-canopy harvest corridors.

He governs by slowing escalation, preserving route boundaries, and sustaining long-cycle canopy balance.

## Beliefs About Them

Senior communities trust Oryn's restraint. Younger groups argue his old boundaries no longer match current pressure.

## Narrative Role

Oryn represents elder authority that protects continuity against fast extraction impulses.

## Notes

His influence is strongest where tribe paths overlap and disputes repeat seasonally.
