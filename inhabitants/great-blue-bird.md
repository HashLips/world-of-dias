# Great Blue Bird

## Overview

Great Blue Bird is the name given to reported giant avian predators said to patrol upper lanes and exposed transit zones near Hearthline corridor edges.

## Description

Accounts describe extreme scale, a dominant blue body profile, and a beak powerful enough to seize adult travelers. Most reports come from distance sightings, disrupted caravan lines, or cautionary retellings.

## Beliefs About Them

For some communities the Great Blue Bird is literal wildlife. For others it functions as protective folklore enforcing child and traveler discipline in hazardous terrain.

## Narrative Role

The figure reinforces Hearthline behavioral norms: stay in marked routes, travel in group cadence, and treat open-zone bravado as avoidable risk.

## Notes

Current canon keeps taxonomic certainty open while preserving strong cultural impact.
