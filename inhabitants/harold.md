# Harold

## Overview

Harold is a runner of Veloria City who has lost nearly everything in life; running is the one thing no one can take from him.

## Description

Each morning, Harold wakes and begins his long run around the city. Because Veloria is so massive, a full circuit cannot be completed in a single day. Instead, he treats the route as a continuous multi-day loop and returns to it as the core routine of his life.

For Harold, running provides daily freedom and clarity. The act itself is both survival and self-definition.

## Beliefs About Them

People who know Harold often describe him as a figure of stubborn resilience: a man stripped down to motion, discipline, and purpose.

## Narrative Role

Harold grounds Veloria's vast scale in a human rhythm, showing how a single person can map a city through repeated effort over time.
