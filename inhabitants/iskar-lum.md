# Iskar Lüm

## Overview

Iskar Lüm is the Dune Keeper of Lumira Sands.

## Description

Iskar appears as a drifting, unstable figure rather than a conventional human body, with facial and limb-like forms shifting in relation and scale.

His authority comes from survival knowledge: mapping hidden water, enforcing oasis protocols, and protecting silence-law practice.

## Beliefs About Them

Locals treat Iskar as a necessary desert custodian. Outsiders often read him as a threat or omen due to his form.

## Narrative Role

Iskar represents desert stewardship through altered embodiment and disciplined secrecy.

## Notes

His water-route maps are treated as oath-protected knowledge.
