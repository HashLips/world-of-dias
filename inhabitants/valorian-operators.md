# Valorian Operators

## Overview

**Valorian Operators** are what inhabitants of Veloria City call those who tend the central core—named for the **operator** role above all else. Their **only job** is to **keep the city’s hypertech centerpiece running smoothly**. They live in the **innermost zone**—the small, immensely powerful circle where technology from **multiple realms** converges to generate and sustain power for Veloria and the wider realm.

## Description

Day to day, each **Valorian Operator** performs whatever tasks the core requires: monitoring, calibration, repair, and coordination of systems that are beyond the understanding of most who live in the outer rings. Their work is specialized and all-consuming; it defines their place in the city’s social and spatial layout.

## Beliefs About Them

Residents of the outer rings often regard Operators with a mixture of dependence, distance, and rumor—few outsiders fully grasp what happens in the core.

## Narrative Role

They embody the link between Veloria’s ancient-feeling periphery and the realm-scale machinery that keeps the city and F432’s present stability possible.
