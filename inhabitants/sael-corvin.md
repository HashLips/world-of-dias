# Sael Corvin

## Overview

Sael Corvin is the Veil Keeper of Averra Isle.

## Description

Sael governs through selective revelation, deciding which messages and artifacts leave Averra and which remain sealed in cloister archives.

He is often described as distant, bearded, and partially obscured, with rare appearances marked by reported golden tears.

## Beliefs About Them

Mainland observers suspect narrative manipulation. Devotees believe Sael prevents dangerous knowledge from becoming commodity.

## Narrative Role

Sael represents controlled memory and the moral tension between preservation and concealment.

## Notes

Golden tears are frequently interpreted as signs of memory-fracture events.
