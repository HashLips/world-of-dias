# Fruit Walker

## Overview

The Fruit Walker is an elderly wandering figure said to appear unpredictably across F432, most often in Wabet and Nauw, while rarely entering Sorel.

## Description

Accounts describe an old man traveling on foot with baskets of fruit. He is usually calm, speaks little, and appears without warning in settlements or at isolated homes.

Most reports place him along routes tied to Wabet produce movement into Nauw, where Lucky Fruit belief is already culturally strong. His sightings in Sorel are rare enough that many there dismiss the figure entirely.

## Beliefs About Them

People disagree on whether the Fruit Walker is real, misidentified, or a shared tale repeated across generations. Some claim direct encounters; others insist no reliable proof exists.

A persistent belief says that if he arrives at a specific door, it is never random. The visit is interpreted as a sign that an unusual turn is coming to that household.

## Narrative Role

The Fruit Walker functions as a roaming threshold figure in F432 lore: part traveler, part omen-bearer, linking ordinary fruit trade to moments people later describe as meaningful fate.

## Notes

When the figure is said to appear, he may offer Lucky Fruit directly to the household he visits.
