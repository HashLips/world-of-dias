# Veyrin

## Overview

Veyrin are a human-like race in F432 distinguished by one innate **signature trait** per individual.

## Description

Veyrin anatomy is broadly humanoid, but each person manifests a stable uncommon capacity (for example reflective memory, interval pattern sensing, pressureless balance, or tonal tracing). These traits are usually narrow and situational rather than unlimited power systems.

Visually, many Veyrin pass as human at first glance, then show one persistent marker linked to their trait: mirrored irises, shifting vein-light, pattern-scored skin lines, or a subtle halo-like distortion around hands, voice, or gaze during focused use.

## Beliefs About Them

They are often admired, overestimated, or mistrusted depending on context; Veyrin communities emphasize that trait discipline matters more than trait spectacle.

## Narrative Role

Veyrin support character-driven storytelling where personal difference matters without forcing all conflict into combat-scale abilities.

## Notes

Veyrin traits vary person-to-person and should be treated as specific competencies with limits, costs, and training.
