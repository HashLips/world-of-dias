# Leora Finn

## Overview

Leora Finn is the Harvest Warden of Glasswater Fields.

## Description

Raised in dam-watch culture, Leora built her authority by organizing harvest protection and night vigilance during sky-panic periods.

Her leadership style is calm, practical, and care-centered, with emphasis on shared continuity over rapid expansion.

## Beliefs About Them

Field communities see Leora as an anchor of both morale and logistics. Detractors argue her caution slows growth in high-demand cycles.

## Narrative Role

Leora represents stabilizing stewardship where food security is treated as communal duty.

## Notes

Her public image emphasizes composure even under threat.
