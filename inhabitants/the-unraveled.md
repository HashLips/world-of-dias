# The Unraveled

## Overview

**The Unraveled** is the name most often whispered in song, oath, and tavern when speakers mean **inhabitants of the F500 abyss**—a frequency where **place itself fails to hold**, and where **bodies, names, and intentions** are said to **come apart in ways that are not quite death**.

## Description

**What witnesses claim (inconsistent)**  
No reliable portrait exists. The few overlapping threads compare the Unraveled to **motion without mass**, to **a mouth opened where there is no head**, to **a crowd made of a single thread pulled loose**, or to **a presence that is only a sudden wrongness in peripheral vision**—a feeling that the dark **has noticed you and chosen not to show its face yet**.

**Why the name**  
“Unraveled” implies **a thing that was woven** (identity, time, a soul-line) and is now only **a trailing edge**. The name is an insult, a prayer, and a warning at once.

**Certainty**  
Across F432 and wider Dias gossip, there is **no shared proof** of Unraveled biology, language, or society. The category remains **a rumor-bucket** for everything F500 is feared to keep.

## Beliefs About Them

Some traditions insist the Unraveled are **punitive spirits**; others, **natural void fauna**; still others, **misinterpreted void phenomena** with no will at all. The entries contradict each other by design: F500 is a frequency that **eats certitude**.

## Narrative Role

The Unraveled are a **creepy, flexible antagonist-verb** for stories about the abyss: a name to invoke when a tale needs **dread without explanation**.

## Notes

**Status: rumor**—safe to use as folklore, fear-name, and ambiguous silhouette; **do not** treat as confirmed zoology. For visual work, lean **almost-no-form**: **hint, smear, wrong proportion**, not a clean monster design.
