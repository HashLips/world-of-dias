# Aurel-kind

## Overview

**Aurel-kind** is the common label for the **energy-native** inhabitants of F200, most often encountered along **Aurel Meridian**—beings whose bodies and boundaries read as **stable light, tuned vibration, and intentional geometry** rather than flesh in the ordinary sense.

## Description

**Visual (for art reference)**  
Figures are typically described with **a core filament** (a line or spindle of gold-white) surrounded by a **soft corona** and **slower rings of honey-colored interference**. Edges are clean; silhouettes can look **slightly prismatic** when “moving,” as if the outline were a **refraction of self** through calm air. Faces, when present, are often **mask-smooth ovals of light** with no pupils—expression carried by **brightness modulation** and **harmonic shimmer** (a tremolo in the corona) rather than micro-features.

**Behavior**  
Aurel-kind are widely characterized as **serene, deliberate, and non-predatory**. Conflict, when described, is rare and tends to be framed as **dissonance**—a wrong note in a shared field—rather than as territorial violence. Social gathering can resemble **lattice patterns** in the air, or a **row of bright nodes** along the meridian’s length.

**Limits**  
This record does not yet specify governments, rites, or true population counts; the Aurel-kind remain **culturally and physically distinct** from F432 life.

## Beliefs About Them

Outsiders sometimes mythologize the Aurel-kind as **saints of frequency**; others as **alien harmonics** that only mimic personhood. Neither claim is established.

## Narrative Role

Aurel-kind function as a **lived proof** that Dias supports **ecologies of light** and **high-coherence personhood** outside material biology.

## Notes

Contrast **Aurel-kind** with the **rumored** F500 **Unraveled** when building moral or visual binaries (peace vs. absence; clarity vs. negation).
