# Andrew and Russell

## Overview

Andrew and his dog Russell are a familiar inhabitant pair seen on the outskirts of town in the Velorian Basin, often playing ball in open ground.

## Description

Andrew is commonly described as an easygoing local boy, and Russell as an energetic companion who follows him through basin-side paths and field edges. Their most recognized routine is simple: Andrew throws, Russell runs, and they repeat for long stretches of afternoon and early evening.

Though modest compared to larger basin trade stories, the pair is often remembered as a small but vivid part of local life in Nauw.

## Beliefs About Them

Residents speak of them as a reminder that not every meaningful story in F432 is tied to conflict or myth; some are rooted in ordinary affection and daily play.

## Narrative Role

They represent grounded, human-scale joy within a region otherwise known for movement, exchange, and pressure.

## Notes

Sightings are most often reported in outer basin areas rather than in Veloria's inner city rings.
