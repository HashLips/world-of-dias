# Zephan Quell

## Overview

Zephan Quell is the Curator-Lord of The Sketchy Gallery.

## Description

F432 race record: **Choirglass**.

Zephan is an extraordinary and socially magnetic figure known for curating immersive art spaces and carrying multiple performative talents.

One recurring depiction presents him in active struggle with a bull, reinforcing his reputation for theatrical courage.

## Beliefs About Them

Admirers see Zephan as a celebrant of beauty and shared wonder. Skeptics argue he favors spectacle over restraint.

## Narrative Role

Zephan represents soft cultural authority built from taste, gathering, and aesthetic intensity rather than formal rule.

## Notes

He is strongly associated with high visitor engagement and social vitality in the gallery district.
