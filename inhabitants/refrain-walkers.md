# Refrain Walkers

## Overview

Refrain Walkers are F120 wayfinders trained to navigate recurring-but-shifted terrain patterns.

## Description

They track difference between passes with exceptional precision: what repeated, what moved, what changed tone. Their logs are full of paired annotations and variation marks.

## Beliefs About Them

Outsiders sometimes treat them as mystics; F120 sources describe them as disciplined field readers.

## Narrative Role

Useful bridge characters for stories involving echoes, legacy, and near-repetition.

## Notes

Their authority comes from comparative memory, not prophecy.
