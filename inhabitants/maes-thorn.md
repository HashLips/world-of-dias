# Maes Thorn

## Overview

Maes Thorn is the Orchard Warden of Verdant Reach.

## Description

F432 race record: **Human**.

Maes rebuilt failing orchard systems through careful land rotation while enforcing firm boundaries against opportunistic overharvest.

Her distant stare and unclear origin made her one of Wabet's most discussed leadership figures.

## Beliefs About Them

Core growers trust her long-cycle planning. Outsiders and opportunists often fear her and spread stories about hidden abilities.

## Narrative Role

Maes represents productive order held by a leader who is both respected and unnerving.

## Notes

A persistent rumor claims she can fully read minds during negotiation.
