# Vexra Coil

## Overview

Vexra Coil is the Free-Captain of Driftfall.

## Description

Vexra built influence by surviving hostile takeovers, then absorbing rivals into moving alliances and paid crew structures.

Her authority is kinetic: command by momentum, risk, and spectacle.

## Beliefs About Them

Followers call her fearless and adaptive. Detractors say she thrives on chaos because only chaos preserves her advantage.

## Narrative Role

Vexra represents Driftfall's unstable freedom economy, where power flows fastest to whoever moves first.

## Notes

Her network influence spans salvage routes, boat crews, and temporary port markets.
