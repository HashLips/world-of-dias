# Green Flower Keeper

## Overview

The Green Flower Keeper is the resident caretaker responsible for planting, arranging, and maintaining the thousands of potted green blooms at Green Garden Border Lanes.

## Description

Local accounts describe one central keeper role that oversees the entire site, with day-to-day work focused on pot rotation, soil checks, spacing alignment, and light-oriented placement. The keeper's discipline is what makes the display look like a natural meadow despite being fully hand-managed.

## Beliefs About Them

Travelers often speak of the keeper with unusual respect, treating the role as both gardener and guide. Some say the keeper can read subtle light patterns and adjust rows in advance so the full display shifts smoothly through the day.

## Narrative Role

The keeper personifies a key Nauw pattern: transforming routine care into shared public meaning. Through one person's ongoing labor, a transit border becomes a place of pause and renewal.

## Notes

Formal succession customs for the keeper role are not yet publicly documented.
