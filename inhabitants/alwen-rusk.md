# Alwen Rusk

## Overview

Alwen Rusk is the Second-Beginning Keeper of Hearthvale.

## Description

Once denied shelter by several settlements, Alwen became a builder of durable reintegration systems across west Sorel.

Her leadership model centers on shared tools, mutual labor, and social re-entry for those marked as failures elsewhere.

## Beliefs About Them

Hearthvale communities see Alwen as proof that exile does not end a life. Driftfall critics call her system a softer form of control.

## Narrative Role

Alwen represents restorative authority in a region born from rejection.

## Notes

Her practical legitimacy is strongest in cooperative workshop and food-network zones.
