# Serik Dovant

## Overview

Serik Dovant is the High Steward of Nauw.

## Description

F432 race record: **Human**.

He rose through route arbitration in Brindle March and became known for keeping movement, food flow, and settlement pressure in workable balance.

Serik's method is procedural rather than theatrical: hearing all parties, forcing terms into writing, and treating continuity as the first duty of leadership.

## Beliefs About Them

Supporters see him as Nauw's stabilizer. Critics say he bends too often toward Veloria's priorities.

## Narrative Role

Serik embodies middle-layer governance where peace depends on negotiation more than force.

## Notes

His authority is strongest along contested transit and distribution corridors.
