# Vailor Quentin

## Overview

Vailor Quentin is the High Resonant of F432 and a defining authority figure across the realm.

## Description

He is recognized for projecting absolute control and a public image of disciplined stability. His red-sail crown-standard is treated as a visual mark of realm command throughout F432.

In formal assemblies he speaks in short declarative lines, preferring enforceable direction over debate. Even communities that resist him still measure their own political stance against his declared position.

## Beliefs About Them

Most communities accept Vailor as the anchor of order. A persistent minority distrusts him and follows local leaders instead.

Two incompatible stories persist around his crown-standard: one says he seized it by overpowering a Singing Fisherman during his rise, while another says it was gifted as recognition of his realm-binding role.

## Narrative Role

Vailor represents centralized realm power and the constant tension between unity and autonomy in F432.

## Notes

The red-sail crown claim is culturally significant but unresolved as established fact.
