# Tidehearts

## Overview

Tidehearts are attunement specialists in F380 who read and regulate emotional-current conditions across the Bloomline Estuary.

## Description

They serve as gatherers, guides, and de-escalators, using ritual cadence, spatial arrangement, and tone management to keep convergence zones livable.

## Beliefs About Them

Some cultures romanticize them as empaths; in-region they are treated as practical civic workers.

## Narrative Role

Tidehearts make F380 actionable in stories: not abstraction, but trained social craft under environmental pressure.

## Notes

Their practice is collaborative and procedural, not mind-control.
