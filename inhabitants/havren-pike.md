# Havren Pike

## Overview

Havren Pike is the Gate Lord of the DEB Gate of Veloria.

## Description

F432 race record: **Kharad**.

Though simple in apparent stature, Havren is regarded as a high-threat defensive commander with a strict threshold doctrine.

He is known for circular blade weapons and for enforcing legitimacy at Veloria's main gate corridor.

## Beliefs About Them

City residents often view Havren as a necessary protector. Border critics argue his gate discipline can become political exclusion.

## Narrative Role

Havren represents armored civic order: the figure who turns symbolic architecture into active security law.

## Notes

Traditions state he previously served as a knight in the Lorien army before rising to Gate Lord.
