# Veil Scribes

## Overview

Veil Scribes are F610 recorders trained to capture structural truths during short reveal windows.

## Description

They use high-discipline notation protocols to log what appears during reveal phases and what disappears afterward, producing layered records with confidence levels.

## Beliefs About Them

Some praise them as truth keepers; others criticize them for overvaluing brief clarity.

## Narrative Role

They support plotlines where evidence is real but fleeting.

## Notes

Veil records are always phase-stamped to prevent false certainty.
