# Elyra Mossen

## Overview

Elyra Mossen is the First Keeper of Wabet.

## Description

Formerly a river-path guide, Elyra gained trust by refusing high-yield extraction terms that would have damaged soil and water continuity.

She leads as a coalition keeper rather than a central ruler, binding tribe elders to shared limits instead of a strict command chain.

## Beliefs About Them

Many Wabet communities view Elyra as a guardian of living systems. Opponents argue she sacrifices growth and leverage for principles.

## Narrative Role

Elyra represents restraint as power: governance that protects future habitability over short-term gain.

## Notes

Her authority is strongest where water access, harvest boundaries, and tribal passage rights intersect.
