# Singing Fishermen

## Overview

Singing Fishermen are seafaring inhabitants associated with Veloria and the Nauw Outer Rim Seas, known for using sung calls in navigation, coordination, and whale encounters.

## Description

They are the primary people who encounter human-eyed whales at scale. Some crews hunt whales for food and continuity of supply across settlements. Others maintain long-term friendships with specific whales, swimming beside them, communicating through repeated sonic patterns, and refusing to hunt bonded companions.

This creates a dual practice inside the same tradition: relational partnership with some whales and active hunting of others.

## Beliefs About Them

They are alternately regarded as protectors, providers, and morally conflicted specialists who carry the burden of decisions made at sea.

## Narrative Role

The Singing Fishermen personify the unstable boundary between intimacy and necessity in Nauw's ocean economies.
