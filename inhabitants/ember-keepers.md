# Ember Keepers

## Overview

Ember Keepers are the long-line stewards of F840's Hearthline corridors, responsible for maintaining viable warmth, shelter, and relay continuity.

## Description

Their work is repetitive and essential: stabilizing heat nodes, marking safe passes, rotating rest stations, and handing forward practical knowledge.

## Beliefs About Them

They are widely respected as proof that care can be infrastructural.

## Narrative Role

Ember Keepers anchor post-crisis stories in durable human (or near-human) effort rather than spectacle.

## Notes

Their authority is earned through maintenance record and trust, not charisma.
